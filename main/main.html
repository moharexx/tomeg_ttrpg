Lets start with the basic explanation of Dice!
Dice are called d#, where # is the amount of sides the dice have.
For example d6 is a 6 sided dice, 8d6 is the sum of 8, 6 sided dies' values.
But if you see d6 √ó 8 that is the result of only one d6 multiplied by 8, totally different!

Essence

Essence, the basis of the T√∂megpokalipszis, all there is consists of it, and everything is in relation to it.
Essence is the amount of charge of a Meta-Concept, which are like abstract Platonic Forms of building concepts.
Usually appearing in the form of particles, we call these Essence Particles, or just Essence.
Each Particle holds some amount of Essence, so charge(s) of a Meta-Concept, even multiple different types at different intensities at once.
While raw Essence and Essence Particles are different, distinction is often unnecessary as only the amount of charge matters.

There are infinite unique different Meta-Concepts, but scholars seperated them into 7 different main groups.

Magic

Magic is what makes the T√∂megpokalipszis, reality manipualtion fueled by Essence.
To use up Essence, one must instruct commands to the universe, and pay up with the charge.

First, one must make instructions of what is to happen.
The way of making these instructions up determines a style of casting.
Scholars split these styles into 3 main groups. Wizardry, Psionics, and Sorcery.

Wizardry (INT Casting) is all about precise definitions, like coding, often creating the spell well before using it.
Psionics (WILL Casting) is like a skill, it comes from focus and feedback.
Sorcery (EGO Casting) is about your character, your desires, and self expression.

Then you pay up with the Essence, either ripping it from around you, or using your stored reserves.
The Essence Circutry in your Soul and Body make the Magic happen as the Essence's charge is used up.

There are "magics" often not called Magic, like the natural Essence enhance of strong warriors, and such.
These also use Essence in the same way, often being Psyonics (WILL Casting), but sometimes else.
Some use a hybrid way of casting, where their Magic is a mishmash of different casting styles.
