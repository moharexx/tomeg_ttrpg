Hulls

This is the base of the Vehicle, this section encompasses its size, weight, speed, and Movement Type(s).
Hulls have a Tier Level, like many things, this is a general quality indicator, and the main scaling factor for the Hull's stats.
A Hull has a Weight Class, and a Type of Movement, these determine its Stats.
There are also Augments the Hull can have, like Arms.

Base Market Price of Hulls: 100 × Tier
Hulls also have 5 × Tier Armor VS HP.
In the table below; Weight, Size, HP, STR, and Attachments Slots scale with Tier Level.

Industrial Grade Weapons

Industrial Grade (I.G.) Weapons are Weapons too big to be used personally, and must be Mounted to something or Wielded by a Vehicle.
These are mostly like regular Weapons, and have 8 Property Points, but there are some differences.
Using when not Mounted or not Wielded by a Vehicle gives Disadvantage, unless STR is at least 30 × Tier

Industrial Grade Weapon Stat
Market Price 100 × Tier
Weight 10Kg × Tier

There are 2 Types of Industrial Grade Weapons; Mounted and Wieldable.
These Types also interact differently with Melee and Ranged.

I.G. Weapon Types:

  • Mounted: These must be Mounted to a Vehicle or Terrain, Taking 2 × Tier Attachment Points from a Vehicle.
  • Wieldable: These will require to be Wielded by a Vehicle to be used.

Melee I.G. Weapons:

  • When Mounted; replace the Attack made on Vehicle Collisions.
  • When Wieldable; it can be a Shield, with 6 Property Points.

Ranged I.G. Weapons:

  • When Mounted; Range is Double that of regular Ranged Weapons.
  • When Mounted; the Weapon can only shoot once per Round, or Twice if it's a Light Weapon.
  • The Aim Property can only be used on Wieldable Types.

Industrial Grade Curios

Industrial Grade (I.G.) Curios are Special Curios too big to be used personally, and must be Mounted to a Vehicle or Terrain.

Modifications

Hulls, I.G. Weapons and I.G. Curios can have a few special modifications.

Miniaturization:

  • Sometimes you don't want your Vehicle to be too big, but you still want that potency and quality.
  • With this the select Hull, I.G. Weapon, or I.G. Curio can be much smaller and lighter.
  • For 3× Market Price the Weight and Size of the Part is reducible to if it was only Tier 1.
