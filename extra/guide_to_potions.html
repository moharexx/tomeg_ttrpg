POTIONS

Potions are (often) a Single-Use container of a Spell.
Players may get to possess Potions from many sources, from Finding, to Purchasing, or even Creating them.

A Potion has an Ability Stored in them. Depending on the Type of Potion the Ability might come out in different ways on usage.
A Potions rarity is mainly dependant on the ESS of the Ability stored inside.

Potion Creation

Drinkable Potions

The most common Type of Potion.
When this Type of Potion is Consumed, the Ability Stored Procs on the Consumer.

These Type of Potions can only Store Spell Type Active Abilities.
Self, Touch, Ranged Target Select, and Projectile Abilities are Casted directly on the Consumer.
Cone and Line Abilities can be used after Drinking the Potion, like a dragon breathing out the Ability.
Sphere AOE Abilities are casted directly Centered on the Consumer, with Maximum Damage on the Consumer.

Usage Stats:

  • Use Time: Bonus-Action

Creation:

  • Creation Cost (Bonus-Action): 20 × ESS
  • Creation Cost (Main-Action): 50 × ESS
  • Creation Cost (Main-Action + Bonus-Action): 100 × ESS

Splash Potions

Also a common Type of Potion.
This Potion is Thrown, and the Ability Stored Procs on the Hit Target.
The Throw counts as a Throw Attack, and On-Attack Abilities can be put on it.

These Type of Potions can only Store Spell Type Active Abilities.
If the Ability is a Projectile, the Potion will become the Projectile.
Self, Touch, Ranged Target Select, AOE, and Projectile Abilities are Casted directly on the Hit Target.
Cone and Line Abilities will be cast in the direction of the Potion's vector.

Usage Stats:

  • Use Time: Main-Action
  • To-Hit: Same as Throwing a Finesse Weapon
  • Range: Same as Throwing a Finesse Weapon

Creation:

  • Creation Cost (Bonus-Action): 5 × ESS
  • Creation Cost (Main-Action): 15 × ESS
  • Creation Cost (Main-Action + Bonus-Action): 40 × ESS
  • Creation Cost (2 Main-Action): 100 × ESS

Weapon Oils

This Type of Potion isn't as common as the rest.
This Potion is applied to the Target Weapon or Ammunition.

These Type of Potions can only Store Any Type of On-Attack Abilities.
The Type of Attack still must obey the Activation Requirement of the Ability.
Abilities which consume a kind of Resource cannot be used.
When Applied to a Melee Weapon, it will Proc the On-Attack on all Attacks made with the Weapon for a set Duration.
When Applied to Ammunition, it will be on it till the Ammunition is used.

Usage Stats:

  • Use Time: Main-Action + Bonus-Action
  • Duration (Melee Weapon): 1 Minute (20 Rounds)
  • Duration (Ammunition): Till Used
  • Ammunition Coated on Use: 2

Creation:

  • Creation Cost (Bonus-Action): 40 × ESS
  • Creation Cost (Main-Action): 100 × ESS
