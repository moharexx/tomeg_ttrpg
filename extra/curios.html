Curios

Curios are items with special properties, appearing as anything from weapons, to armors, to trinkets, to implants.
It's scope isn't limited to just "magic", Curios can be technological marvels, like a laser sword for Tiered Armament.
An item could have multiple Curio Effects, when that's the case, all Curio Effects are looked at separately!

To be able to use a Curio, you must have them somewhere on your person, and be currently Attuned to them.
Attunement is an Interact Action (Bonus-Action)
Each time Attuning you can Un-Attune and Attune to any number of Curios at once.

Tiered Armament

The most basic Type of Curio, a Tiered up Armament.

Tiers increase the Base Stats of Armaments:

  • Base Damage Dice, Armor, Armor-Pen, and HD Gain are Multiplied by: Tier Level
  • Range of Ranged Weapons are Multiplied by: (Tier Level)^(1/3)

There are 2 Subtypes of Tiered up Armaments Types; Channeling, and Full.

Channeling:

  • This Armament takes on the Tier of the User's ESS.
  • But there is a Cap, based on the Curio's Tier, if the Curio isn't strong enough you can't Channel such power through it.
  • This Cap is the Curio's own Tier.

Full:

  • These Armaments are the same as the Channeling Type, but can use their own Tier, if it's higher.
Type Market Price
Channeling 150 × Tier
Full 300 × Tier

Ability Type

Ability Type Curios are used to Cast an Ability, or use a Passive Ability.
These cannot themselves be just a Meta-Ability, and you cannot use your own Meta-Abilities on them!

This Type of Curio has a Minimum Price if the Ability or it's Unlock has a very high XP Cost.
Also every Unlock of the Ability adds a little extra Price.

There are 2 Subtypes of Ability Types; Channeling, and Full.

Channeling:

  • These Curios, when used, use the Caster's ESS, Base Stats, and Masteries.
  • But there is a Cap, based on this Curio's Tier, if the Curio isn't strong enough you can't Channel such power through it.
  • Curio Cap: Tier ESS, and 20 × Tier Base Stats and Masteries.

Full:

  • These Curios are the same as a Channeling Type, but can use their own Stats, if they are higher.
  • Curio Stats: Tier ESS, and 10 × Tier Base Stats and Masteries.
  • Note: This means a To-Hit Modifier of 5 × Tier
  • While you're Attuned to this Type of Curio, it takes 2 × Tier PROXY.

Modifications:

  • Internal Cooldown: The Ability's Cooldown is placed on the Curio rather than the User.
  • Internal Resource: The Ability's Resource Costs aren't applied to the User, and the Curio itself has 0 PROXY Cost.
  • Meta-Ability: Adds a Meta-Ability to the Cast.

Reset Essence Artifact

Unbound; Limitless; Priceless; Unique.
These items break the rules of the game, from little things to complete insanity.
Many who may own one, don't even knows how significant it is.
The complexity ranges from very simple to full unique abilities.

When it can do an Ability, it's mostly a Channeling Type, but rarely it could be a Full Type.
If it's a Weapon, it's also mosty "Channeling", where it's Tier is the User's ESS.
These Artifacts are at the EP (Essence Cap or Tier Cap) of the Omniverse, or even a bit higher. (~1e85)

There are 4 Classifications:

  • Lesser: Despite the name, it's still a priceless Artifact. Countless exist, sought after by those who know.
  • Medium: Many exists, a few circulate, unknowingly shaking up societies of entire universes.
  • Greater: Tens to a hundred; All have been collected by those at the highest echelons.
  • Supreme: A Miracle!
