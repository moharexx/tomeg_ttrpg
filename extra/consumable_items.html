Consumable Items

Consumable Items are made to be used in the pinch of combat, and as the name suggest, are single use.
They do NOT require Wielding Slots to be used!
This means you don't need to Switch Weapon to use, only have it at hand, on a belt, ect...

Scrolls

Scrolls are Items which store a single Cast of an Ability with the Item's own ESS.
Scrolls can appear in many forms for enhanced flavor, from regular scrolls, to imbued gems, to tatoos, to runes, to whatever trinkets.
Scrolls can store any kind of Ability, even modified with Meta-Abilities, but any Ability Cast from a Scroll will be count as Spell Type.
Even On-Attacks, Reactions, and Stances can be stored in Scrolls.

Abilities Cast through a Scroll can be modified further at the moment of Cast by your own Magic Items and Meta-Abilities.

The Scroll's Cast Time is the same as the Ability's, modified normally by any Meta-Ability.
Scrolls can have up to 2 × ESS extra Potency, increasing Market Price by 10C per Point.
For Resource Costs, the Scroll has it built in, and the User doesn't have to Spend any Resources.

Potions (WIP)

Potions are Items which store a single Cast of an Ability with it's own ESS.
Potions can appear in many forms for enhanced flavor, from regular potions, to imbued gems, to whatever trinkets.
Potions can store any Active Ability, even modified with Meta-Abilities, but any Ability Cast from a Potion will be count as Spell Type.

The Ability's Range is modified to be the Potion's destination.
One way of using a Potion is an Interact-Action (Bonus-Action) to consume, or make someone willing in Natural Melee Reach consume the Potion.
The other way is to make a Melee or Ranged Attack with it, like if it was a Finesse Throwable Light Melee Weapon.
For directional AOE, when consumed the User tells the Orientation, when used as an Attack it goes in the direction of the Attack.

Potions can have up to 2 × ESS extra Potency, increasing Market Price by 10C per Point.
For Resource Costs, the Potions has it built in, and the User doesn't have to Spend any Resources.

Bombs

Bombs are items that work like Melee Weapons with the Light, Throw and Finesse Properties. (2 Property Points)
They can be thrown with using the Attack-Action (Bonus-Action), like any other Light Throwable Weapon.

After thrown it Explodes at the Targeted location.
This is a Ranged Flow Sphere AOE Attack, eminating from the Bomb, Targeting everything within the Radius.
If something blocks it from reaching the Target location it still Explodes, where ever it was stopped.

Bombs can be found in many Damage Types, some Types of Damage are Exotic, and are a bit weaker to compensate for their usefulness.
Note: As a Ranged Attack, Enemies which have you in their Melee Reach are Attacked with Disadvantage!
As it's a Melee Weapon, you can Attack with it in Melee, ignoring the Ranged Penalties, but you're automatically Hit.

Delayed Bombs

Delayed Bombs are like Bombs, but count as having 4 Property Points.
Before being Thrown they need to be Primed, this is an Interact Action. (Usually a Bonus-Action)
When Priming choose an Explosion Delay from 1 Round to 20 Rounds (1 Minute), and if it Explodes at the Start or End of Your Turn.
Delayed Bombs can be Thrown just like Bombs (Attack-Action, Bonus-Action), Falling Rules effect Delayed Bombs!

After Explosion Delay Rounds, it will Explode at the designated part of Your Turn.
This is a Melee Flow Sphere AOE Attack, eminating from the Bomb, Targeting everything within the Radius.

Bombs can be found in many Damage Types, some Types of Damage are a bit weaker to compensate for their usefulness.
If the Delayed Bomb remains unthrown, still with the User, and Explodes, the User is automatically Hit!

Effect Vials

Effect Vials are items that work like Melee Weapons with the Light, Throw and Finesse Properties. (2 Property Points)
They can be Thrown with using the Attack-Action (Bonus-Action), like any other Light Throwable Weapon.

These Vials are Single Target, and upon Hitting their Target grant the Effect stored in them.
This Effect by itself does nothing, it's purely for interacting with Features of other Entites and Abilities.

Note: As a Ranged Attack, Enemies which have you in their Melee Reach are Attacked with Disadvantage!
As it's a Melee Weapon, you can Attack with it in Melee, ignoring the Ranged Penalties.

Effect Flask

Effect Flasks are like Effect Vials, but AOE, and count as having 4 Property Points.
They can be Thrown with using the Attack-Action (Bonus-Action), like any other Light Throwable Weapon.

This is a Ranged Flow Sphere AOE Attack, eminating from the Flask, Targeting everything within the Radius.
Upon Hitting a Target, it grants the Effect stored in the Flask.
This Effect by itself does nothing, it's purely for interacting with Features of other Entites and Abilities.

Note: As a Ranged Attack, Enemies which have you in their Melee Reach are Attacked with Disadvantage!
