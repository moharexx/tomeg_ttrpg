Tension

Requirements:

  • Skill: 200 Melee Weapon | 200 Martial Arts

Unlock Cost:

  • 200 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Defense
  • Weapon Damage, HD
  • Main-Action + Bonus-Action / Continuous

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Tension Stance.
While in Tension Stance you can perform different Tricks.
If you don't have the Stacks to Spend you cannot do the Trick.
These Tricks count as Abilities, unless said otherwise.
Max Stack: 5

While in this Stance:

  • Tension Gain: Negating an Attack with HD, which has a To-Hit of at least 5 × ESS gives 1 Stack.
  • Tension Gain's To-Hit Threshold is Halfed While taking the Defense-Action.
  • Constricting Darkness: While under a Darkness Effect, you cannot gain Stacks with Tension Gain.
  • While in Tension Stance if you Take Damage, you lose 1 Stack, you cannot have less than 0 Stacks.

Brighten (Bonus-Action):

  • Spend 1 Stack, then emit Bright-light in a 5 × ESS^(1/3) meter Radius and Low-Light in Double the Radius.
  • Entities inside the Bright and Low-Light Radius gain the Light Effect on a Failed VIT Save of 10 × ESS
  • The Light Effect does nothing on it's own, it's for Entity Interactions, and Caster always Fails the Save.
  • Brighten's Duration is 1 Minute (20 Rounds), the Light Effect Ends when Brighten Ends.

Graze (Free-Action Reaction):

  • Spend 3 Stacks, If you don't have enough HD to Negate an Attack, you gain 15 × ESS HD, which is instantly usable.

Sword and Shield (Bonus-Action):

  • Spend 2 Stacks, then use the Defense-Action.
  • For the purposes of Abilites, Shields, and other Actions you use the Defense-Action as a Main-Action.

Axe (Bonus-Action):

  • Spend 2 Stacks, then make an Attack-Action with 2 × ESS Potency.
  • Potency spent on To-Hit and Armor-Pen is Doubled.

Scraf (Bonus-Action):

  • Spend 2 Stacks, then Heal 5 × ESS an Entity you can Sense within 10 × ESS^(1/3) meters.

Ring (Bonus-Action):

  • Spend 2 Stacks, then Target an Entity which you can Pinpoint within 10 × ESS^(1/3) meters.
  • The Target takes 2d6 × ESS Cold Damage on a Failed VIT Save of 10 × ESS, or Half as much on a Successful one.

TensionMax (Bonus-Action):

  • Spend 3 Stacks, then gain 5 Stacks.

The Stance Breaks only when entering any other Stance.
