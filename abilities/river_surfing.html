River Surfing

Requirements:

  • (20 INT | 20 WILL | 20 EGO), (20 Conjuration | 50 Enchantment | 200 Evocation)

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Physical Damage, Mobility
  • Main-Action + Bonus-Action / Bonus-Action

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in River Surfing Stance.
While in River Surfing Stance, you cannot use the regular Movement-Action.
Once on your Turns, including the first Turn of the Stance, as a Free-Action you can Surf 3 × CS^(1/3) meters.
This Stance has two triggers, you can choose one from below.
If the caster gets Damaged while in River Surfing Stance, the Caster will get the Prone Condition.

Barrel Roll:

  • You move 6 × CS^(1/3) Amount, this Movement does not Activate Reactive Abilities, or Trigger Stances.
  • When using this Stance Trigger, you cannot use the Free-Action Surf provided by the Stance.

Board Kick:

  • You make an Attack against an Entity within 6 × CS^(1/3)
  • When using this Stance Trigger, you cannot use the Free-Action Surf provided by the Stance.
  • Wet Effect: On Dealing Damage, the Target gets Wet with Water.

The Stance Breaks when: Taking Damage
