Description

You imbue your Melee Attack with Fire.
On a Hit you inflict the Wound Effect.

Wound Effect:

  • At the Start of the Caster's Next Turn the Target takes (1d8 × ESS) Physical Executing Damage, with 10 × ESS Armor-Pen.
  • This Effect Ends after Dealing Damage.
