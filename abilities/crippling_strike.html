Crippling Strike

Requirements:

  • Skill: 10 Melee Weapon | 10 Martial Arts | 50 Ballistic

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Debuff, Crowd Control
  • Bonus-Action

Description

When using this Ability, the Parent Attack can apply the Rooted Condition.
If the Attack Deals Damage, the Target must Succeed on a STR Save, or get the Rooted Condition.
The Rooted Condition lasts until the End of the Target's Next Turn.
