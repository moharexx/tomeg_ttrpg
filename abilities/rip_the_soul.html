Rip The Soul

Requirements:

  • Spell: (20 INT | 20 WILL | 20 EGO), (10 Anima | 20 Enchantment | 50 Abjuration)

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Targeting, Soul Save
  • Main-Action

Description

When used, you rip out the Soul of a Pinpointed Entity or more.
You have Targeting Points equal to 4 × ESS, you can spend Targeting Points on Entities to Target them.
The Soul Save's Difficulty is equal to the Targeting Points spent on the Target.
On a Failed Save the Target's Soul is ripped out, floating right above their Body.
Yes, this is basically an instakill effect if the Soul cannot get back somehow...
