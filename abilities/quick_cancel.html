Quick Cancel

Requirements:

  • Skill: 20 INIT | 20 acrobatics | 20 intuition

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Defense
  • Self, Negation
  • Bonus-Action

Description

When you Trigger a Reaction or Stance, you can Cancel your Action which did that.
This means your Action, Movement, or Ability never happened, but you do NOT get back the Action Cost of it.
The Reaction or Stance you Triggered still goes off, possibly wasting it.

If your INIT + (10 × ESS) is less than their INIT, then you Fail to Cancel in time, and this Ability does nothing.
