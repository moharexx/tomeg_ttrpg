Sense Essence

Requirements:

  • Soul or similar

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Utility, Unlock
  • Sense

Description

You can Sense Essence within 10 × Perception Modifier^(1/3) meters.

Essence Clarity (20 XP):

  • You can Pinpoint Essence within 10 × Perception Modifier^(1/3) meters, and Sense in Double the Range.

Sensing Essence opens up a new world, from the auras of living things, to the glows of towns and forests far away.
Every Soul interacts with Essence, breathing it, shaping it, using it.
Natural phenomena also interact with Essence, like rivers, forests, air currents, and even the sun.
Essence glows, flows, lingers, and acts in ways where all senses become applicable in detecting it in their own way.
You can see or hear an ability being cast, or smell that it was cast, or even taste it on the surface of where it was cast.

The Ranges assume 1 ESS / m3
For weaker/smaller or stronger/larger essence clusters the Range of Sensing may vary.
Exact Ruling up to GM.

Most Entities don't have this Passive Ability Unlocked.
