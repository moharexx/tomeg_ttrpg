Heartbreak

Requirements:

  • Spell: 20 CS, (10 Enchantment | 20 Evocation | 50 Abjuration)

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Ranged, VIT Save, Physical Damage, Debuff
  • Main-Action + Bonus-Action

Description

When using this Ability, you burn a Pinpointed Entity away from the inside.
The Target must make a VIT Save of 12 × ESS or take 2d6 × ESS Physical Damage.
On a Failed Save the Target also takes the Dazed Condition till the End of their Next Turn.
