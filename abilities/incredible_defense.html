Incredible Defense

Requirements:

  • Skill: 10 STR | 10 AGI

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Defense
  • Negation
  • Bonus-Action, GRIT | HD

Description

You Spend either GRIT or HD to use the Defense-Action as a Bonus-Action.
For the purposes of Abilites, Shields, and other Actions, treat as if you used the Defense-Action as a Main-Action.
When using this Ability, even after Un-Wield Your Shield, You still gain the full benefits of the Defense-Action.
The ESS you Cast this Ability at is Total XP / 100, and if somehow it's less, the Ability Fails.
