Aurora Blink

Requirements:

  • Spell: (20 INT | 20 WILL | 20 EGO), (10 Transmutation | 20 Evocation | 50 Enchantment)

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Heat Damage, AGI Save, Static Pillar AOE, Mobility
  • Main-Action

Description

When used, You Blink and release a pillar of aurora where you land.
This is a Static Pillar AOE Melee Attack.
When arriving to your new location, Entities inside the Pillar must succeed an AGI Save of 8 × ESS or take Heat Damage.

COMBO:

  • If you Damage an Entity with this Ability, The Ability's Cast Time will be lowered to a Bonus-Action Untill the End of your Next Turn.
  • Damaging Entities with less Total XP than 10 × ESS put into this Ability will not Activate COMBO.
  • Aurora Blink's Movement does NOT Activate Reactions, or Trigger Stances.
