Coneify (Shape WIP)

Requirements:

  • 500 Total XP

Unlock Cost:

  • 50 XP

Tags:

  • AOE +
  • AOE -

Description

You Change an AOE into a Cone AOE, the new Cone AOE will retain it's Flow Type.
Depending on the AOE you Change into a Cone, the Cone Length will Differ.
The Cone's Spread is 90° (1 Forward = +2 Width)
The Cone starts out from the Caster.

AOE to Cone Length Table:

  • Sphere -> Cone Length = 2 × Radius
  • Line -> Cone Length = Length / 10
  • Cylinder | Disk -> Cone Length = Height / 5 | 2 × Radius
  • Cube -> Cone Length = Side Length

You can Change a Cone's Spread, the Cone's Length will be Multiplied by some amount for each Jump
Increasing the Spread counts as -1 Jump, and Decreasing the Spread counts as +1 Jump, Jumps can be a Negative Value.
You can cut off from the Start of the Cone, and bring it's start closer to the Caster by the same amount, effectively Truncating the Cone into a Frustum.
You can Flip the Cone, to where it starts wide and shrinks into a point at the end.

Cone Length and Spread Table:

  • 30° (~3 Forward = +2 Width)
  • 60° (~2 Forward = +2 Width)
  • 90° (~1 Forward = +2 Width)
  • 120° (~1 Forward = +3 Width)
  • 180° (Half Sphere)
  • Overridden 30° -> 180° Length Multiplier: 0.1
