Whiplash

Requirements:

  • Skill: 10 Melee Weapon | 10 Ballistic | 10 Martial Arts

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Defense, Utility
  • Range, STR Save, Mobility, Crowd Control
  • Bonus-Action

Description

When you use this Ability, you throw out a rope or just force air to act like it, and pull on a Hit Target.
Make a Ranged Attack, use any Martial Mastery to determine To-Hit, on a Hit you can Pull yourself towards the Target.
Alternatively you can try to Pull the Target towards yourself, on a Failed STR Save of 8 × ESS the Target is Pulled.
The Direction of the Pull cannot curve, and must remain straight!
Additionally, if you Hit a Target, you can choose to not Fall at the End of Your Turn.
