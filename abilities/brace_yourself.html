Brace Yourself!

Requirements:

  • Innate: 50 INT | 50 WILL | 50 EGO
  • Spell: (5 INT | 5 WILL | 5 DES), (5 Abjuration | 10 Anima | 20 Enchantment)

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Defense
  • Self, Negation
  • Bonus-Action / Continuous

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Brace Yourself! Stance.
While in Brace Yourself! Stance, you gain (6 × ESS) Psychic, Radiant, Necrotic Resitance, and make Soul Saves with Advantage.

The Stance Breaks when: Using Abilities, Moving, HD Decrease

Note: This Ability CANNOT CRIT.
