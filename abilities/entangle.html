Entangle

Requirements:

  • Spell: (5 INT | 5 WILL | 5 DES), (5 Conjuration | 10 Abjuration | 20 Enchantment)

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Static Cube AOE, Crowd Control
  • Main-Action / Continuous

Description

Within Range, in a choosen Cube, vines and roots grow out, entangling everything in the area.
When using this Ability, you are put in Entangle Stance.
Entities at the Start of their Turn Inside the Area must make a STR Save or get Slowed until the Start of the Casters Next Turn.
If the STR Save was Successful, the Slowed is Halved.

  • Targets reduced to 0 MS count as Rooted.

The Stance Breaks only when entering another Stance.
