Description

You make your Melee Attack so precise, it hits twice.
On a Hit you put a DoT Effect on the Target.
This Effect will Trigger at the Start of Caster's Next Turn.
The DoT will repeat the Hit of the Parent Attack.
Meaning that the DoT is like an Attack with infinite To-Hit.
The DoT deals the same Damage as Parent Attack, allong with all of its On-Attacks (other than this).
The DoT Effect Ends after Dealing Damage.
