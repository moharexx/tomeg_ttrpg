Darkness

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Utility, Unlock
  • Obscurance, Crawling Sphere AOE
  • Main-Action

Description

When used, you create a Sphere of Darkness, eminating from a Point.
On Your Turns as a Free-Action you can Move the Point which the Darkness eminates from by 5 × CS^(1/3)
Entities who Enter or Start their Turn in Darkness must Succeed a VIT Save of 14 × ESS or have the Darkness Effect.
The Darkness Effect does nothing on it's own, it's for Entity Interactions.

Shaped Darkness (50 Transmutation):

  • This Ability can be Shaped in whatever way the Caster desires.

Targeted Darkness (20 Anima):

  • You can Cast Darkness onto a Pinpointed Target.
  • Target's are eligable to a Soul Save of 10 × ESS to Cancel the Ability.
  • This Darkness has Half the Radius, and moves with the Target.
