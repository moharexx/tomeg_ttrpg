Animate Dead

Requirements:

  • Spell: 20 CS, (10 Anima | 20 Enchantment | 50 Conjuration)

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Defense, Utility
  • Proxy
  • Main-Action + Bonus-Action

Description

When used, a Target corpse in Range gets Animated as an Undead.
The Undead has Undying, Essence Sustenance, and Dark Vision.
The Undeads have (6 × ESS) Radiant Weakness

Each Undead under Control occupies (10 × ESS) Proxy
Undead understand their owner's language, and obey commands.
Undead can think and have memory (based on the INT stat), so they can follow plans and formations.
The Caster can forgo Control as a Free-Action, this doesn't make the Undead Perish.
The Caster loses Control if the Distance is greater than 1000 × CS^(1/3) meters for over a Day.
Undead without Control will wander and be hostile to anything alive.
Defeated Undead are in too bad of a Condition to be used again. (Unless repaired somehow.)

The form and type of Undead is determined by the corpse.
Fleshy bodies make a Zombie, and their Base MS will be overridden to 3 × ESS^(1/3)
Skeletal bodies make a Skeleton, which have (6 × ESS) Bludgeon Weakness
Limbs are based on the corpse, and the Undead can have the Multi Legged Passive.
The Undead are able to wear Armor, and if they have the appropriate limbs, even Wield Weapons and Shields.
Claws and jaws are considered Natural Melee Weapons with 1d8 Base Damage, 5 Armor-Pen, and Tier of this Ability's ESS.
