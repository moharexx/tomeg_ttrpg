Description

When using this Ability, you make a Melee Attacks against a Target.
If the Target gets 0 or less HP from this Attack, the Target must make a VIT Save of 10 × ESS or get the Unconscious Condition.
The Unconscious Condition lasts until the End of a Short-Rest.
This Ability has Executing.
