Glow Glitter

Requirements:

  • Spell: (5 INT | 5 WILL | 5 EGO), (5 Enchantment | 10 Divination | 20 Evocation)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Static Cube AOE, Sense
  • Main-Action

Description

You choose an Area, glowing glitter emerges and covers everything.
Targets in the Area can make a (VIT | Soul) Save of 10 × ESS, or get the Light Effect.

Light Effect:

  • Targets have a faint glow, revealing them in Darkness, and Invisibility.
  • Lasts 1 Minute (20 Rounds).
