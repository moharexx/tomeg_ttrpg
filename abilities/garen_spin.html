Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Garen Spin Stance.
While in Garen Spin Stance, you make a Melee Weapon Attack against Entities inside your Reach.
The Casters MS is also halved while in this Stance.

The Ability Flows out from the Caster.
This Ability has the same Armor-Pen as the Wielded Weapon it is used with.
