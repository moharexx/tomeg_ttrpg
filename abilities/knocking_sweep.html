Knocking Sweep

Requirements:

  • Skill: 10 Melee Weapon | 20 Martial Arts

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Defense
  • Melee, Flow Sphere AOE, STR Save, Crowd Control
  • Main-Action, GRIT

Description

When using this Ability, you attempt to knock Entities away from yourself.
Entities inside your Melee Reach must succeed a STR Save of 10 × ESS or be Knockbacked.
The Direction of the Knockback is directly aways from you.

  • You can Spend 5 × ESS GRIT to change the Knockback direction for each Entity.
