Rush

Requirements:

  • 10 Athletics

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Variable Damage, Mobility
  • Main-Action + Bonus-Action

Description

You Rush to a Target in a Straight Line, and make a Melee Attack-Action | Grapple | Shove against it.
If you are Sprinting, Double the Rush Distance.

  • When Rushing to a Target, the Target must end up inside your Melee Reach
  • Rush's Movement cannot curve.
