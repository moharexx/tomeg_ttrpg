Fiery Trail

Requirements:

  • Skill: 50 Acrobatics
  • Spell: 10 CS, (10 Evocation | 20 Transmutation | 50 Conjuration)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Heat Damage, Static Special AOE
  • Bonus-Action

Description

When using this Ability, you leave a trail of fire behing yourself.
When moving you leave Fiery Trail behind Yourself.
On AOE Abilities that move you, you leave Fiery Trail in the AOE of the Ability.

Fiery Trail:

  • Fiery Trail is a Static Special AOE, it has the same Height and Width as you.
  • Fiery Trails Lenght is it's Parent Movements Lenght.
  • The Fiery Trail lasts for 1 Minute (20 Rounds)
  • Entities Standing in the AOE take 1d12 × ESS Heat Damage.
  • Fiery Trail does not Stack, and the Strongest one will be in Effect.
