Tidal Burst

Requirements:

  • 10 CS, (5 Conjuration | 10 Transmutation | 20 Evocation)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Defense
  • Physical Damage, BUFFER
  • Main-Action + Bonus-Action / Bonus-Action

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Tidal Burst Stance, and surround yourself with a water bubble.
When entering Tidal Burst Stance, you gain (20 × ESS) BUFFER.
On Stance Trigger, you make an Attack in a Cone, converting half your BUFFER into Physical Damage.

  • The BUFFER must be from this Ability for it to deal Damage.
  • The Stance doesn't Trigger if you have BUFFER from other things.

The Stance Breaks when: Using Abilities, Moving, HD Decrease

Soaked Effect:

  • On Dealing Damage, the Target gets wet with water.
  • This Effect Ends after the Entity uses a Main-Action + Bonus-Action to remove it.
