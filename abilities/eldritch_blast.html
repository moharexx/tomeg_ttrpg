Eldritch Blast

Requirements:

  • Spell: (10 INT | 10 WILL | 10 EGO), (5 Evocation | 10 Abjuration)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Unlock
  • Ranged, Demat Damage, Projectile
  • Main-Action

Description

When used, fire a Single Target Projectile made of force.
You can customize this spell with Unlocks.

Agonizing Blast (20 Evocation, 5 XP):

  • You can add your CS / 2 to the Spells Damage.
  • This makes this spells Cast Time bigger by a Bonus-Action.

Distant Blast (20 Divination, 5 XP):

  • The Spells Range is 30 / 60 × CS^(1/3).
  • This makes this spells Cast Time bigger by a Bonus-Action.

Piercing Blast (20 Abjuration, 5 XP):

  • The Spell has (10 × ESS) Demat-Pen
  • This makes this spells Cast Time bigger by a Bonus-Action.

Critical Blast (50 Enchantment, 10 XP):

  • You can Increase the Spells CRIT Range by 1 for 5 × ESS SP Cost.
  • The Max SP you can Send is 10 × ESS
  • This can be Applied even after the Attack's Roll.

Tormentor's Blast (50 Anima, 10 XP):

  • You can use the Ability as a Stance, with a Free-Action Trigger on your Next Turn.
  • The Blast will be shot out at the Stance Trigger.
  • After Hitting a Target, the Hit will be repeated at the Start of Your Next Turn, along with all of it's On-Attacks.
  • The Stance Breaks when: Using Abilities, Moving, HD Decrease, Taking Damage

Eldritch Smite (50 Melee Weapon | 50 Martial Arts, 10 XP):

  • The Ability becomes a Melee Attack with Melee Reach and it's Damage is (2d6 × ESS) Demat
  • If Distant Blast is Active: Reach is Increased by CS^(1/3)
