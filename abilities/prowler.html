Prowler

Requirements:

  • 10 Stealth

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Utility
  • Buff
  • Main-Action + Bonus-Action / Continuous

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Prowler Stance.
While in Prowler Stance, your Stealth d10 Checks get a Bonus of 5 × ESS
While in this Stance, your CRIT Range is Increased by 4 Against Unaware Entities.

The Stance only Breaks when entering any other Stance.
