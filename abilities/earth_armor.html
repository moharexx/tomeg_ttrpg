Earth Armor

Requirements:

  • Spell: (10 INT | 10 WILL | 10 EGO), (5 Conjuration | 10 Transmutation | 20 Enchantment)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Defense
  • Negation, Touch
  • Main-Action

Description

When used, Cover yourself with Enhanced Earth, which Protects from Physical Attacks.
Unwilling Entities cannot be Targeted.
While the Armor is on, MS is Halved, but gain (6 × ESS) Armor, this protects from Physical Damage. (Slashing, Piercing, Bludgeoning)

  • If an Attack Hits with more than (20 × ESS) Premitigated Damage the Earth Armor Shatters, and the Ability Ends.
  • Against Thunder Damage, or while Soaked with water, the Break Threshold is Halved.

Note: This Ability cannot CRIT! duh!
