Clairvoyance

Requirements:

  • Spell: (10 INT | 10 WILL | 20 EGO), (5 Divination | 10 Anima | 20 Enchantment)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Utility, Unlock
  • Sense
  • Bonus-Action

Description

Choose a Point, you create an invisible incorporeal Sensing object at that Point.
Clairvoyance has the same Senses as you do.
Clairvoyance can be Pinpointed with a Successful Scan Area (Intuition | Perception) of CS
As a Bonus-Action you can Move the Clairvoyance 4 × ESS^(1/3) meters
As a Bonus-Action you can refresh the Duration of Clairvoyance.

Distant Clairvoyance (10 XP):

  • Cast Clairvoyance for 1 Minute (20 Rounds) to increase the Spawn Range to 2000 × ESS^(1/3)
  • When you Cast it this way you can specify a vague description to where Clairvoyance should Spawn.
  • Example: "I want to see a busy street!" -> Spawns in a highly populated street.
  • When there is nothing matching your description, Clairvoyance Fails.
