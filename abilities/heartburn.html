Heartburn

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Offense

Description

When using this Ability, you burn a Pinpointed Entity away from the inside.
The Target must make a VIT Save of 12 × ESS or take 1d12 × ESS Heat Damage and Support Reductions.
The Damage of this Ablity is repeated at the Start of Casters Next Turn.
All Healing, Shielding, and BUFFERs the Target recieve is Reduced by 6 × ESS until the End of their Next Turn.
