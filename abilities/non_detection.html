Non-Detection

Requirements:

  • Skill: 50 Stealth
  • Spell: (10 INT | 10 WILL | 20 EGO), (10 Enchantment | 20 Divination | 50 Anima)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Utility, Unlock
  • Obscurance
  • Bonus-Action

Description

You Touch your Target, and it turns Non-Detectable to a Selected Sense.
Perception based on that Sense cannot Pinpoint the Target, unless Passive Perception is at least than 10 × ESS
Using any Ability with the Offense Tag will Break the Non-Detection.
Targets are eligable to a Soul Save of 14 × ESS to not be effected.
When you use this Ability as a Skill, it's Range is Self.

When you Unlock this Ability you can choose 1 of the 10 XP Senses down below for Free.

Senses:

  • Invisibility (Sight): 10 XP
  • Silence (Hearing): 10 XP
  • Odorlessness (Smell): 10 XP
  • Essence Cloak (Essence Sense): 10 XP
  • Every other Sense in one: 20 XP

Full Non-Detection:

  • As a Main-Action you can apply Non-Detection to all Senses you Unlocked in this Ability, at once.

Phantom Detection (50 Divination)
You can designate Entities who will be able to Sense you normally.

Superior Non-Detection (20 XP)
Non-Detection does not Break, but it's Duration becomes Until End of Target's Next Turn.
