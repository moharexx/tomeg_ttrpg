Telepathy

Requirements:

  • Spell:(10 INT | 20 WILL | 50 EGO), (5 Divination | 10 Anima | 20 Transmutation)

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Communication, Targeting

Description

You can Telepathically and Empathically Communicate with Entities you can Sense within Range.
With a Successful Soul Save of 10 × ESS the Target can block Communications for up to 1 Minute (20 Rounds)
Your Target knows who they are Communicating to, unless you use a Feint Action.
The Target can attempt a Scan (Arcana or Intuition) Check against the Feint.
