Blood Spear

Requirements:

  • Skill: 20 Martial Arts
  • Spell: (10 INT | 10 WILL | 10 EGO), (5 Transmutation | 10 Enchantment | 20 Anima)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Ranged, Flow Line AOE, Physical Damage, Executing
  • Main-Action, REST-HP

Description

When used, fire a Spear made of Blood, in a straight Line.
This is a Flow Line AOE Ranged Attack, flowing out from the Caster.
This Ability has Executing.

Soaked Effect:

  • On a Hit, the Target gets Soaked with "water".
  • This Effect Ends after the Entity uses a Main-Action + Bonus-Action to remove it.
