Magic Savant

Requirements:

  • Innate: 10 of any Magic-Mastery

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Buff, Mastery

Description

Your Mastery in Magic allow you to enhance yourself in different ways.
You can assign a single unique Buff to all of your Magic-Masteries.
You cannot choose the same Buff or Magic-Mastery more than once!
After a Long-Rest you can reassign what Magic-Mastery gives what Buff.

Reinforced Body:

  • Your Base Max HP increases by (Conjuration | Enchantment | Abjuration) / 2
  • The new Max HP is Capped at Total XP / 5

Reinforced Soul:

  • Your Base Max SP and Proxy increases by (Conjuration | Abjuration | Anima) / 2
  • The new values are both capped at 3 × (Total XP / 10)

Reflex Surge:

  • Your Base Max HD increases by Evocation | Transmutation | Divination
  • The new Max HD Cap depends on Armor Weight Class; Light 3 × (Total XP / 10), Medium 2 × (Total XP / 10), Heavy Total XP / 10

Precognition:

  • Your Base INIT increases by (Evocation | Divination | Anima) / 2
  • The new INIT is Capped at Total XP / 10

Swift Movement:

  • Your Base MS increases by (Evocation | Transmutation | Enchantment)^(1/3)
  • The new MS is Capped at 2 × (Total XP / 10)^(1/3)

Fortified Firewall:

  • One of your Save's Modifier increases by (Abjuration | Divination | Anima) / 2
  • The new Modifier is Capped at Total XP / 20

Life Reserve:

  • Your Base REST-HP increases by(Transmutation | Conjuration | Enchantment) × 2
  • The new REST-HP is Capped at 4 × (Total XP / 10)
