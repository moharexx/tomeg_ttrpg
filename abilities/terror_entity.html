Terror Entity

Requirements:

  • Innate: 10 Performance
  • Spell: (5 INT | 5 WILL | 5 DES), (5 Enchantment | 10 Anima | 20 Divination)

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Soul Save, Targeting, Crowd Control
  • Main-Action

Description

When used, you Terrify a Pinpointed Entity or more.
You have Targeting Points equal to 12 × ESS, you can spend Targeting PointsThe Soul Save's Difficulty is equal to the Targeting Points spent on the Target.
On a Failed Save the Target gain the Terror Effect.

  • If Cast as Innate, the Ability's Range is 5 × Performance Modifier^(1/3)

Terror Effect:

  • Entities have Disadvantage on Attacks against the Caster and cannot willfully move closer to the Caster.
  • This Effect Ends if the Caster gets the Dying Condition or Dies.
  • The Terror Effect Last for the Abilites Duration.
  • Entities at the End of Their Turn can repeat the Soul Save, Ending the Terror Effect on a success.
