Description

When used, fire Two Blazing Lightnings Projectile, in a straight Line.
Both Projectiles deal both Heat and Electric Damage.

Electric Effect:

  • Against Airborn Targets this Ability Deals Maximalized Damage.

Heat Effect:

  • Against Entities Wet with Water, this Ability Deals Half-Damage.
