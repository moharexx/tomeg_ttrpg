Fiery Spiral

  • Spell: (10 INT | 10 WILL | 10 EGO), (5 Evocation | 10 Conjuration | 20 Transmutation)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Heat Damage, Flow Disk AOE
  • Main-Action + Bonus-Action / Main-Action

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Fiery Spiral Stance.
When entering this Stance, you lift yourself up with Fire up to CS^(1/3) × 2 meters, and start hovering.
While in this Stance, you eject a jet of Fire downwards which disperses in a Disk Shape on the Ground.
At the Start of the Stance and Start of your subsequent Turns Entities touching the Disk must Succeed a VIT Save or take Damage.
When the Stance is Triggered, you smash down, and Entities in the Disk must Succeed a VIT Save or take Increased Damage.
Even on the Turn in which the Stance is Triggered, the Hover Damage still normally procs on the Turn's Start.

The Stance Breaks when: Using Abilities, Moving
