Description

When used, you Touch an Entity and Remove the Unconscious Condition.
If the Entity has less then 1 HP, It will have the Dying Condition.
If the Unconscious Condition is caused by a Spell then this Ability will Attempt a Save against the Spell's Save.

Note: If the Soul Left the Body, the Spell Fails.
