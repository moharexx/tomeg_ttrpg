Description

"When the Stars Align..."
When using this Ability, you are put in When The Stars Align Stance.
You predetermine in which direction the Beam will go on Trigger, this direction is obvious to any observer.
While in When The Stars Align Stance your Rolled INIT counts as 0 for the purpose of Round Order.
The Next Turn you can Trigger the When The Stars Align.
On Trigger you shoot out a massive Beam of pure power, this is a Flow Line AOE Attack.
After the Initial Attack, the Line Travels it's Length at the Start of every Round, repeating the Attack, for 9 Rounds.

The Stance Breaks when: Using Abilities, Moving, HD Decrease
This Ability CANNOT CRIT.
