Chain Lightning

Requirements:

  • Spell: (20 INT | 20 WILL | 20 EGO), (10 Evocation | 20 Conjuration | 50 Transmutation)

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Electric Damage, Crawl Line AOE
  • Main-Action + Bonus-Action

Description

When used, fire a Bolt of Lightning, which can be bent multiple times.
This is a Crawl Line AOE Ranged Attack, that Hits Multiple Targets, but not Multiple times any single Target.
This AOE Attack Crawls around Targets, meaning a Full Negation will not Stop the Line's AOE.

Line Bending:

  • The Lightning Bolt can be bent into a different direction 3 Times in it's Travel.
  • This makes a Max of 4 Segments, with their combined Length not greater than: 20 × ESS^(1/3)

Electric Effect:

  • Against Soaked, or Airborn Targets this Ability Deals Max-Damage.
