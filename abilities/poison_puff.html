Poison Puff

Requirements:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Ranged, Targeting, Bio Damage, VIT Save
  • Main-Action

Description

When used, you create a puff of Poison at a Pinpointed Entity or more.
You have Targeting Points equal to 10 × ESS, you can spend Targeting Points on Entities to Target them.
The VIT Save's Difficulty is equal to the Targeting Points spent on the Target.
On a Failed VIT Save, the Target takes Damage equal to the Targeting Points spent on the Target.
