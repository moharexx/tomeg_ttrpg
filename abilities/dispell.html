Description

When used, a Magical Effect must be Targeted.
If the Target Magical ESS or Tier is less than Dispell Strength, then the Target Ends.

Self Dispell:

  • Dispelling any Spell Cast by the Caster is a Free-Action, and always Succeeds.

Dispell Potency:

  • For each 5 Points of Potency the Ability is Cast at, increase Dispell Strength by 1.
