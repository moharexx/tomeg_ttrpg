Description

Part of your mind works with computer precision and speed.
You can store data, and retrieve it, this is fully lossless, making you remember things perfectly.
You can store up to (INT | Divination) GB of data this way.
You can also run programs, with a Clock Speed of (INT | Divination) MH
Use the x86 or ARM architecture, or make up whatever fantasy architecture, I don't care.
With any Telepathic Ability you can also connect to computers, or any other machines with an I/O Interface or Network Protocol.
