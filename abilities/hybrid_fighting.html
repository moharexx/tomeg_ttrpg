Hybrid Fighting

Requirements:

  • Innate: 5 STR, 5 AGI

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Mastery

Description

The To-Hit Modifier of Weapons can be ((STR | AGI) + Melee Weapon + Martial Arts) / 4, to a Maximum of Total XP / 20
For Attacks and Abilities with Finesse Weapons, their Main Stat can be the sum of STR and AGI, to a Maximum of Total XP / 10
For being Proficient in using Finesse Weapons (not having Disadvantage), add together your Melee Weapon and Martial Arts Masteries.

For the Maximum Mastery you can put into Melee Weapon and Martial Arts, add together your STR and AGI, to a Maximum of Total XP / 10
For the purpose of adding to HD, you add together your Melee Weapon, Martial Arts, and Ballistic Masteries, to a Maximum of Total XP / 10
The MS formula can benefit from both STR and AGI; 2 × (STR + AGI)^(1/3), to a Maximum of 2 × (Total XP / 10)^(1/3)

Note: This Ability cannot result in higher stats than a maxxed character without it, hence every formula is limited by Total XP.
So like if someone maxxes STR and Melee Weapon, you cannot have higher To-Hit at the same Total XP with this Ability.
