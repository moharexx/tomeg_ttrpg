Hybrid Fighting

Requirements:

  • Innate: 5 STR, 5 AGI

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Buff, Mastery

Description

Melee Weapon and Martial Arts Modifiers can be (Melee Weapon + Matrial Arts) / 2, Capped at Total XP / 10
For Attacks and Abilities with Finesse Weapons, their Main Stat can be STR + AGI, Capped at Total XP / 10

Rather than choosing only 1 Martial-Mastery to add to Base HD, add them together, Capped at Total XP / 10
MS benefits from both STR and AGI; 2 × (STR + AGI)^(1/3), Capped at 2 × (Total XP / 10)^(1/3)
