Hyper Sleep

Requirements:

  • Innate: 20 VIT, 50 WILL

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Self, Buff, Debuff
  • Free-Action

Description

When used, you gain the fall into deep sleep and your body and soul starts to recover.
For the Duration of this Ability, you have the Unconscious Condition.
While this Abiltiy is active, your Perception is 0 and none of your Senses are active.
When the Duration of this Ability expires, you gain the benefits of a Long-Rest.
If the Unconscious Condition Ends Early, you gain no benefits from this Ability.
