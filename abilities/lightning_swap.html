Lightning Swap

Requirements:

  • Skill: 10 Melee Weapon | 10 Ballistic | 10 Martial Arts

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Action Economy
  • Free-Action, GRIT | HD

Description

You spend either GRIT or HD to use Switch-Weapon or Interact-Action as a Free-Action.
For the purposes of Abilites and other Actions treat as if you used the Switch-Action or Interact-Action as a Main-Action.
The ESS you Cast this Ability at is Total XP / 100, and if somehow it's less, the Ability Fails.
