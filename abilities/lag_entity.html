Lag Entity

Requirements:

  • Spell: (20 INT | 20 WILL | 20 DES), (10 Enchantment | 20 Divination | 50 Anima)

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Soul Save, Targeting, Crowd Control
  • Main-Action

Description

When used, you Lag a Pinpointed Entity or more.
You have Targeting Points equal to 8 × ESS, you can spend Targeting Points on Entities to Target them.
The Soul Save's Difficulty is equal to the Targeting Points spent on the Target.
On a Failed Save the Targets gain the Lag Effect.

Lag Effect:

  • The Target cannot use Reactions.
  • Every Action the Target takes in their Next Turn will happen only at the Turn after that.
  • After the Actions finally come out for real, the Target takes no Actions that Turn.
  • On the 3rd Turn the Target is no longer Effected by Lag.
  • Lag has NO Effect on Entities in Monolithic Style.
