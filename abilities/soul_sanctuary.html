Soul Sanctuary

Requirements:

  • Innate: 50 SP

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Defense
  • Negation

Description

When your SP gets below - Max SP, rather than your Soul instantly shattering, it goes into a safe state.
While in this state you are Unconscious, and taking a Long-Rest.
If you gain back enough SP to be at least at -(Max SP), this state Ends, and you gain back consciousness.

Your Soul can still shatter, but only if your SP goes below Total XP / 2
