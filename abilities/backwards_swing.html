Backwards Swing

Requirements:

  • Skill: 10 Melee Weapon

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Cooldown
  • Weapon Damage
  • Bonus-Action

Description

When using this Ability, you make a Melee Attack-Action with Disadvantage.

Forwards Swing (50 INIT, 10 XP):

  • As part of some Abilities you make a Melee Attack-Action, when doing so you can Forward Swing it.
  • A Forward Swinged Attack's Action Cost is reduced, but gains Disadvantage.
  • From Main-Action to Bonus-Action, or from Bonus-Action to Free-Action.
  • Using Forwards Swing doesn't count as an Ability for Breaking Stances.
  • Forwards Swing has a 1 Minute (20 Round) Cooldown.
  • Example of Abilities that can be Forwards Swinged: Retaliate, Haymaker, Opportunity Attack, Riposte.
