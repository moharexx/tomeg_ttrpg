Description

When using this Ability, you make a Melee Attack-Action with Disadvantage.

Forwards Swing (50 INIT, 10 XP):

  • When you make a Melee Attack-Action as a Reaction or Stance Trigger, you can use Forwards Swing.
  • Forwards Swing is just like Backwards Swing bit with a 1 Minute (20 Round) Cooldown.
  • Forwards Swing does NOT Break Stances with the Using Abilities Stance Break.
