Slayer Style

Requirements:

  • Skill: 20 STR, 20 WILL

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Style
  • Melee, Ranged

Description

Entering And Leaving the Fighting Style:

  • Once per Turn, as a Free-Action, you can commit to Entering | Leaving this Fighting Style.
  • This means you Enter | Leave the Fighting Style at the Start of Your Next Turn.

Slayer Style Pros:

  • All Weapons you use are considered Finesse, even Ranged and Heavy weapons.
  • You can use STR as a Base Stat for Ranged Weapons.

Slayer Style Cons:

  • You cannot use Spells, only Innate and Skill Type Abilities can be used.
