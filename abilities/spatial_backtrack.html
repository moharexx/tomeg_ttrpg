Spatial Backtrack

Requirements:

  • Spell: (20 INT | 20 WILL | 50 EGO), (10 Conjuration | 20 Transmutation)

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Self, Soul Save, Mobility
  • Main-Action / Bonus-Action

Description

When used, you save your current position, and can return to it later.
As a Bonus-Action you can Recast to teleport back to the saved position.
You can choose additional Targets, if they are Pinpointed, are in Backtrack Radius, and their combined mass is not more than Max Mass.
Unwilling Targets can make a Soul Save to not be Teleported.

  • You can only have 1 Spatial Backtrack at a time!
  • If you walk out of Backtrack's Range then the Ability Ends.
  • If the saved position is somehow blocked, like being in solid ground, then the Recast Fails.
  • This Ability's Movement does NOT Break Stances.

Multiple Backtrack (20 Anima):

  • You can have multiple Spatial Backtracks.
  • For each Spatial Backtrack after the first, you must supply 10 × ESS Proxy.
