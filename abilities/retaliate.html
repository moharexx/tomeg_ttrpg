Retaliate

Requirements:

  • Skill: 20 Martial Arts | 50 Melee Weapon

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Defense
  • Melee, Weapon Damage, Negation
  • Main-Action + Bonus-Action / Trigger

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Retaliate Stance.
While in Retaliate Stance, the next Attack Ignores your HD, and Hits you.

  • When Hit, if Retaliate-Strength is Equal or Greater than Incoming Pre-Mitigation Damage, you Retaliate the Attack.
  • When you Retaliate an Attack, you Negate the full Attack, meaning Effects, ect...
  • When you Retaliate an Attack, you can also Teleport to the Attacker if they are within 10 × ESS^(1/3) Range, and make a Melee Action-Attack.
  • Retaliate's Teleport does NOT Activate Reactions, or Trigger Stances.
  • If the Attack-Action would cost you only a Bonus-Action you make this Attack with Advantage.
  • You can also activtivate Retaliate by succeeding a save.
  • When you Retaliate a Dash Type Attack, you can choose to stop or not to stop the Dash of the Attack.

The Stance Breaks when: Using Abilities, Moving, HD Decrease, Taking Damage
