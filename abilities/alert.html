Alert

Requirements:

  • Skill: 10 Perception, 10 Intuition

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Self, Buff
  • 1 Minute / Continuous

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Alert Stance.
While in Alert Stance Perception and Intuition Checks gain a Bonus of 10 × ESS and INIT is increased by 10 × ESS

  • This Ability Stays on even when Unconscious.

The Stance Breaks only when entering any other Stance.
