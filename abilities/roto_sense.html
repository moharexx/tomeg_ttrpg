Roto Sense

Requirements:

  • Innate: 10 Perception

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Sense

Description

You have a Sense that allows you to Rotoscope everything within (Perception Modifier)^(1/3) meters.
You feel material around you as a kind of fuzzyness.
This means you can Sense everything material around you, including in and over solids.

Roto Pinpoint (20 Perception, 10 XP):

  • This Ability can Pinpoint, and Sense in Double the Radius.
