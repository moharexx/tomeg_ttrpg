Refresh

Requirements:

  • 20 WILL

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Cooldown -
  • Resource Cost +

Description

You use SP to ignore the Cooldown of an Ability.
When you use Refresh, an already present Cooldown does not update or go away.

Refresh SP Cost:

  • 1 Minute (20 Rounds) Cooldown: 4 SP × ESS
  • 1 Hour (1200 Rounds) | Short-Rest Cooldown: 8 SP × ESS
  • 1 Day (28800 Rounds) | Long-Rest Cooldown: 12 SP × ESS
