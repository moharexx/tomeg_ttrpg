Weapon Whip

Requirements:

  • 10 Melee Weapon

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Crowd Control
  • Bonus-Action

Description

When using this Ability, you Attack the Target Entity.
If the Attack Hits, the Target must succeed on a STR Save, or get Moved.
If the Target is Airborn, the Forced Movement is doubled.

Yes, you can knock people airborn.
