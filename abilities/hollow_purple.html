Description

"Nine Ropes, Polarized Light, Crow and Declaration, The gap within and without..."
"Hollow Purple"

When using this Ability, you are put in Hollow Purple Stance.
The Next Turn you can Trigger the Hollow Purple Slash.
You shoot out a large sphere of exotic matter, dematerializing everything in it's path.
This is a Projectile, Targeting everything in it's path, acting like a Flow Line AOE Attack with a Width.

The Stance Breaks when: Using Abilities, Moving, HD Decrease, Taking Damage
