Out Of My Way!

Requirements:

  • Skill: 10 Athletics | 10 Martial Arts

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Self, Mobility, Crowd Control
  • Main-Action / Bonus-Action

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Out Of My Way! Stance.
While in Out Of My Way! Stance, you can use the active part of Pass Through as a Free-Action.
While in Out Of My Way! Stance, you can use your Full Athletics Modifier for the Save Dificulty.

On Stance Trigger:

  • Till the End of Your Turn, you gain the Benefits below.
  • You attempt a Shove on any Entity you Pass Through.
  • Once, as a Free-Action, you can move up to MS / 2

The Stance Breaks only when entering any other Stance.
