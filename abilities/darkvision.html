Darkvision

Requirements:

  • Skill: 200 Perception
  • Spell: (5 INT | 10 WILL | 20 EGO), (5 Enchantment | 10 Divination | 20 Transmutation)

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Touch, Buff, Sense
  • Bonus-Action

Description

When used, you Touch an Entity and grant it Darkvision.
The Target Treats Low-Light and Darkness as if it were Bright-Light.
Low-Light and Darkness with more ESS than This Ability's ESS are remain dark.

When this Ability is Cast as a Skill it's Range is Self.
