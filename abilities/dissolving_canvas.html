Dissolving Canvas

Requirements:

  • Spell: 20 CS, (10 Conjuration | 20 Transmutation | 50 Enchantment)

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Ranged, Demat Damage, AGI Save, Effect
  • Main-Action + Bonus-Action

Description

When used, you Target a Pinpointed Entity in Range, and attempt to infect them.
If the Target fails a AGI Save of 12 × ESS they gain the Soaked Effect.

Soaked Effect:

  • On Dealing Damage, the Target gets Soaked with Acid.
  • At the Start of the Caster's subsequent Turn the Target takes (8 × ESS) Demat Damage.
  • This Effect Ends after the Entity uses a Main-Action + Bonus-Action to remove it.
