Organizing Tornado

Requirements:

  • Spell: 5 CS, (5 Transmutation | 10 Evocation | 20 Conjuration)

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Ranged, Flow Cone AOE, STR Save, Crowd Control
  • Main-Action

Description

When used, you pull Entities into a line infront of you with a tornado.
This is an AOE Attack, flowing out from the Cone's line of symmetry. (The center line going through the Cone)
Entities inside the Cone on a Failed STR Save of 10 × ESS get pulled to the closes point on the Cone's line of symmetry.
