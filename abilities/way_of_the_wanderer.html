  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Defense

  • Bonus-Action / Free-Action

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Prowler Stance.
While in this Stance, your CRIT Range is Increased by 1
On Stance Trigger your CRIT Range is Increased by 2 till the End of Your Turn.

The Stance only Breaks when entering any other Stance.
