Bastion Shield

Requirements:

  • Skill: 10 Melee Weapon

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Defense
  • Negation, Self
  • Bonus-Action

Description

When Targeted by an AOE while having a Heavy Shield equipped you can use this Ability.
This Ability will change the behaviour of the Flowing or Crawling AOE which Targets the Caster.
You gain extra (6 × ESS) Armor VS All against the AOE.
The AOE when Fully Negated by you, in the Direction of it's Flow, leaves behind a 90° Cone where the AOE does not Reach.

Example: You Reduce a Cone Of Cold to Deal no Damage to you, Entities in a full 90° Cone behind you Will NOT be affected by the Cone Of Cold.
Note: While the Cone Of Cold Example is a Flow AOE, this works same with Crawling AOEs as well.
