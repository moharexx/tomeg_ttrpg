Will Of Armament

Requirements:

  • Innate: 10 WILL

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Buff

Description

Any Weapons, Shield, and Armor you're currently Wielding or Wearing can be treated as having Tier equal to your ESS.
This Tier increase doesn't effect any extra Enchantments the item has, those stay at the same Tier and ESS.

This Essence Enhance can be used in more ways than just combat stats.
Everything you hold, carry, or wear can be much tougher and harder, from clothes, to tools, and more.
You can solidify cloth to hold its shape, or carry something flimsy without it falling apart, or even make the air around you viscous.
