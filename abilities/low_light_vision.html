Low-Light Vision

Requirements:

  • 10 Perception

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Sense

Description

You Treat Low-Light as if it were Bright Light, and Darkness as if it were Low-Light.
Low-Light and Darkness with more ESS than Perception Modifier / 10 are NOT affected.

Darkvision (20 Perception, 10 XP):

  • You Treat Darkness as if it were Bright Light.
  • Darkness with more ESS than Perception Modifier / 10 are NOT affected.
