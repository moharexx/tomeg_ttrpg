Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Demonic Combustion Stance.
On Stance Trigger, you make a Crawl Sphere AOE Melee Save, flowing out from you.
Entities within the Radius must Succeed a Soul Save or take Damage.
This Ability doesn't Damage the Caster.
This Ability has Executing.
While in this Stance, your Rolled INIT for the purpose of Turn Order is 0.

The Stance Breaks when: Using Abilities, Moving, HD Decrease
