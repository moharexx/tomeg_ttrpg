Active Dodge!

Requirements:

  • Skill: 50 AGI | 10 Acrobatics

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Defense
  • Negation, HD
  • Bonus-Action / Continuous

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Active Dodge! Stance.
While in Active Dodge! Stance you can Spend extra 4 × ESS Free HD against Attacks, potentionally making them miss.
You also make AGI Saves with Advantage.
Using this Free HD doesn't Spend from your HD Pool, but still counts as Spending HD for the purposes of Abilities, like Breaking Stances.

The Stance Breaks when: Using Abilities, Moving, Taking Damage
