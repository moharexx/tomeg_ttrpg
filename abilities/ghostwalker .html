Tags:

Description

You imbue your Melee Attack with Ghostly Energy.
On a Hit you deal additional (1d8 × ESS) Psychic Damage and become Transparent until the End of Your Next Turn.
While being Transparent, you cannot be Pinpoint by any Sense, unless Perception Check is at least 10 × ESS
