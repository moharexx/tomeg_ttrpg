Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Shifting Stance.
When Entering this Stance and at the Start of your subsequent Turns, you Teleport 2 × ESS^(1/3) meters.
You can NOT Teleport a collectively weighing more than 50 × ESS Kg
You can NOT Teleport Entities other than Yourself.
On Stance Trigger, you Teleport 2 × ESS^(1/3).
Shifting's Teleportation does NOT Activate Reactions, or Trigger Stances.

The Stance Breaks only when entering any other Stance.
