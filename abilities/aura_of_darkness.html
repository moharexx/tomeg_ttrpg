Aura Of Darkness

Requirements:

  • Spell: 5 CS, (5 Abjuration | 10 Divination | 20 Enchantment)

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Utility, Unlock
  • Obscurance, Any Sphere AOE
  • Bonus-Action / Main-Action

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Aura Of Darkness Stance.
While in Aura Of Darkness, you emit Darkness in a chosen AOE, and Low-Light in Double the Radius.
When Entering Aura Of Darkness Stance, Choose an AOE Type from the table below.
On Stance Trigger, you Double the Radius of the Ability, and will emit Darkness until the End of Your Next Turn.

AOE TypeRadius
Flow8 × ESS^(1/3)
Crawl6 × ESS^(1/3)
Static4 × ESS^(1/3)

Selective Darkness (20 Divination, 5 XP):

  • You can choose which Entities can't see the Darkness.
  • Entities that can't see the Darkness can see it with a Perception Check of at least 10 × ESS

The Stance Breaks only when entering another Stance.
