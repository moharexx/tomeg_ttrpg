Description

You override the Properties of any Skill Type Ability, including Attack-Action, Grapple and Shove.
The Skill or Attack-Action will be treated as if you had Weapon Mastery of CS, Martial Mastery of CS, STR of CS, and AGI of CS
Any Weapon used is Up-Tiered to being Tier of ESS for that Skill.
For each Action Infused with this Power you Spend the SP written in the Table Below.
