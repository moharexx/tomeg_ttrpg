Precognition

Requirements:

  • Innate: 20 Perception | 20 Intuition | 20 Divination

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Defense, Utility
  • Mastery, HD

Description

You see into the immediate future with decent precision.
For the HD Formula you can substitute AGI | Acrobatics | Melee Weapon | Martial Arts with Perception | Intuition | Divination
For the INIT Formula you can substitute AGI | Perception | Intuition with Divination

You cannot substitute multiple parts of the HD Formula, only 1, same with the INIT Formula.
