Holy Ground

Requirements:

  • Spell: 10 CS, (5 Anima | 10 Enchantment | 20 Evocation)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Ranged, Static Sphere AOE, Radiant Damage, VIT Save
  • Main-Action / Continuous

Description

Within Range, in a choosen Sphere secrade light starts to emanate.
When using this Ability, you are put in Holy Ground Stance.
When Entering this Stance and at the Start of your subsequent Turns, Entities in the Radius must Succeed a VIT Save or take Damage.
Within the Radius of this Ability Bright-Light is emitted, and Low-Light in double the Radius, both the Bright and Low-Light are a Static AOE.

Overflow:

  • As a Main-Action, you can choose which Entities are Targeted by this Ability.
  • This can be done only once on this Ability.

The Stance Breaks only when entering another Stance.
