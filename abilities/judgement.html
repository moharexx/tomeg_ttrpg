Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Judgement Stance.
On Stance Triggered, dash in a stright line to a point and Target Entities inside the Radius.
Targeted Entities take 2d10 ×ESS Thunder Damage on a Failed AGI Save of 10 × ESS
The AOE of this Ability is a Melee Flow Sphere, Flowing out from you.

The Stance Breaks when: Using Abilities, Moving, HD Decrease
