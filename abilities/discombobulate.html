Discombobulate

Requirements:

  • Skill: 10 Martial Arts

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Debuff, Crowd Control
  • Main-Action

Description

When used, you make a Melee Attack with a Natural Weapon.
This Deals no Damage, but on Hit, the Target makes a VIT Save.
Targets who Fail the VIT Save will have the Stunned Condition till the End of their Next Turn.

Note: While this Attack doesn't deal any Damage by itself, On-Attacks and other modifiers can add Damage.
