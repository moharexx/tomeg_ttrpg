Master Brawler

Requirements:

  • Skill: 10 Athletics | 10 Martial Arts

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Self, Crowd Control
  • Main-Action / Continuous

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Master Brawler Stance.
While in Master Brawler Stance, you have 1 Free Wielding Slot which can be used only to Grapple a Target.
While in Master Brawler Stance, you can use Grapple or Shove as a Main-Action to gain a Bonus 4 × ESS to the Save Difficulty.
While in Master Brawler Stance, you can Shove a Target as A Free-Action, which will end the Grapple on the Target.
While in Master Brawler Stance, when you Grapple or Shove a Target, you can deal 2 × ESS Physical Damage with 10 × ESS Armor-Pen.
Already Grappled Target can take this Damage, on a Failed STR Save of Athletics Modifier
A Target can only take Damage from this Stance once on a Turn.

The Stance Breaks only when entering any other Stance.
