Master Brawler

Requirements:

  • Skill: 5 Athletics | 5 Martial Arts

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Defense
  • Self
  • Bonus-Action / Continuous

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Master Brawler Stance.
While in Master Brawler Stance, you gain access to 2 new Actions and 1 Free Wielding Slot which can only be used to Grapple a Target.
If a Target Fails a Grapple or Shove STR Save, you can deal 2 × ESS Physical Damage with 10 × ESS Armor-Pen.
Already Grappled Target can take this Damage, on a Failed STR Save of Athletics Modifier at The Start of Your Turns.
A Target can only take this Damage once on a Turn.

Restrain (Main-Action):

  • You can Grapple or Shove with a Bonus 4 × ESS Save Difficulty.

Into The Dirt (Free-Action):

  • You can Shove a Target Grappled by You, which will end the Grapple on the Target.

The Stance Breaks only when entering any other Stance.
