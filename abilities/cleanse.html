Cleanse

Requirements:

  • Spell: (5 INT | 5 WILL | 10 EGO), (5 Abjuration | 10 Enchantment | 20 Transmutation)

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Defense, Utility
  • Ranged, Targeting, Effect
  • Bonus-Action

Description

When used, you Target a Pinpointed Object or Entity in Range, and attempt to Remove an Effect from them.
You select 1 Effect (NOT Condition) to Remove, if the Effect's ESS or Tier is equal or less than this Ability's then it's Removed.
Unwilling Targets are eligible to a Soul Save of 8 × ESS

Potency Boost:

  • For each 2 points of Potency you can Remove +1 Effect from the same Target.
  • For each 2 points of Potency you can Remove 1 ESS or Tier higher Effects.
