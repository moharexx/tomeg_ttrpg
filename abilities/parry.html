Parry

Requirements:

  • 10 Melee Weapon | 10 Martial Arts

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Defense
  • Negation, Debuff
  • Bonus-Action / Trigger

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Parry Stance.
While in Parry Stance, the next Attack Ignores your HD, and Hits you.

  • When Hit, if Parry-Strength is Equal or Greater than Incoming Pre-Mitigation Damage, you Parry the Attack.
  • When Parrying an Attack, you Negate the full Attack, meaning Effects, ect...
  • When Parrying a Melee Attack, the Target gets the Staggered Condition till End of it's Next Turn.
  • When Parrying a Dash Type Attack, you stop the Dash.

Parry Master (20 XP):

  • Parry does not count as a Stance, meaning you can have another Stance Active, and still use Parry.
  • It still counts as an Ability for Breaking Stances.
  • Parry still Breaks normally from all conditions below.

The Stance Breaks when: Using Abilities, Moving, HD Decrease, Taking Damage
