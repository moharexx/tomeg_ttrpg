Yuumi!

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Self, Soul Save, Mobility
  • Main-Action / Continuous

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Yuumi! Stance.
When Entering Yuumi! Stance you attach your self to a Sensed Entity on a Failed Soul Save of 6 × ESS
When attaching to an Entity you choose a point within CS^(1/3) Radius around the target to be at.
You always stay at that relative position around the Target.
You move along with them, and can choose a new position within the Radius.
You can also choose a new position with a Bonus-Action.
An Entity that Failed the Save can repeat it as a Bonus-Action.

The Stance Breaks only when entering any other Stance.
