Quick Recovery

Requirements:

  • Innate: 10 VIT, 10 WILL

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Buff

Description

You recover your Resources quickly.
You can take the Short-Rest in 1 Minute (20 Rounds), and the Long-Rest in 1 Hour (1200 Rounds).
Using Quick Recovery only effects your Resources, and cannot refresh your Cooldowns!

Super Recovery (20 XP):

  • You can take the Long-Rest in 1 Minute (20 Rounds).
  • Quick Recovery still cannot refresh your Cooldowns!
