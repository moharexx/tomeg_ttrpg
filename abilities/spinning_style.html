Spinning Style

Requirements:

  • Skill: (10 Melee Weapon | 10 Martial Arts), 10 Acrobatics

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Defense, Utility, Style
  • Action Economy

Description

Entering And Leaving the Fighting Style:

  • Once per Turn, as a Free-Action, you can commit to Entering | Leaving this Fighting Style.
  • This means you Enter | Leave the Fighting Style at the Start of Your Next Turn.

Spinning Style:

  • Rather than having a Main-Action and Bonus-Action, you have 3 Bonus-Actions.
  • If something would make you lose a Main-Action, you lose a Bonus-Action instead.
