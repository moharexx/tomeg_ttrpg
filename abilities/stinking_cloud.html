Stinking Cloud

Requirements:

  • Spell: (20 INT | 50 WILL | 50 EGO), (10 Conjuration | 20 Transmutation | 50 Enchantment)

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Utility
  • Static Sphere AOE, VIT Save, Debuff, Obscurance
  • Main-Action + Bonus-Action

Description

When used, you create a yellow cloud of nauseating gas, which Sight based detection cannot see through.
Every Entity inside the Radius, at the Start of their Turns are Targeted.
On a Failed VIT Save of 6 × ESS, Targets get the Dazed Condition until the Start of their Next Turn.

A Wind or Shockwave based Attack with at least 20 × ESS Damage disperses the Stinking Cloud.
If that Attack is an AOE then 5 × ESS Damage is enough to disperse the Stinking Cloud.

When a Heat Based Attack Hits the Cloud it quickly Ignites all the connected parts and burns up.
Any Target inside the Area gets dealt 4 × ESS Heat Damage as the Cloud burns away.

Inflammable Gas (20 Transmutation | 20 Enchantment):

  • When Casting this Ability, you can make the Cloud Non-Ignitable.

Shaped Cloud (50 Transmutation):

  • This Ability can be Shaped in whatever way the Caster desires, still fitting inside the Radius.
