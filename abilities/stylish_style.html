Stylish Style (WIP)

Requirements:

  • None

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Defense, Utility, Style
  • Action Economy

Description

Entering And Leaving the Fighting Style:

  • Once per Turn, as a Free-Action, you can commit to Entering this Fighting Style.
  • This means you Enter the Fighting Style at the Start of Your Next Turn.

Stylish Style:

  • You have a Style Quota to fulfill each Turn, if you Fail, the Style Breaks.
  • The Quota starts at 1, and each subsequent Turn increases by +1
  • Keep track of the Total Style you earned.
  • As a Free-Action you can Style Break, this allows you to Spend ALL the Style you earned.

Style Gain Methods (Each Capped at once per Round):

  • Deal Damage | Heal | Shield: +1 Style
  • Reduce Entity to 0 HP | Buff | Debuff: +1 Style
  • Fully Negate an incoming Attack (Armor, HD, Ability): +1 Style
  • Activate a Special Condition: +2 Style

Special Conditions:

  • Special Conditions are when Abilities, Conditions, or Status Effects do some special interaction.
  • Examples: Damaging Someone who is Staggered, Maximizing Executing Damage.

Style Spend Methods:

  • 1 Style -> Recover to 1 HP.
  • 1 Style -> End the Prone Condition.
  • 1 Style -> Make a Move-Action.
  • 2 Style -> Gain a Bonus-Action.
  • 3 Style -> Gain a Main-Action.

Style Break:

  • After Spending your earned Style Points, you gain the Staggered Condition till the End of your Next Turn.
  • You also don't gain back any Actions at the Start of your Next Turn.
  • Note: This is the only way of Exiting this Style!
