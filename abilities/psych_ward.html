Psych Ward

Requirements:

  • Spell: (10 INT | 10 WILL | 10 EGO), (5 Abjuration | 10 Anima | 20 Enchantment)

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Defense, Cooldown
  • Negation, Shielding
  • Free-Action

Description

When used, create a protective mental Ward on Yourself.
When Taking Damage which goes into SP, treat the Psych Ward as a buffer.
The Psych Ward has it's own Resistances, not inheriting yours!
When the Ward's SP hits 0, the Spell Ends, and all remaining Damage goes into you, counting your Resistances.

  • This Ability counts as Shielding for the purpose of other Abilities.

Note: This Ability cannot CRIT.
