Flooding Field

Requirements:

  • Spell: 10 CS, (5 Conjuration | 10 Transmutation | 20 Evocation)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Ranged, Static Sphere AOE, Physical Damage, VIT Save, Crowd Control
  • Main-Action / Continuous

Description

Within Range, in a choosen Sphere the air becomes extremely humid, and small water bubbles will start to from.
When using this Ability, you are put in Flooding Field Stance.
When Entering this Stance and at the Start of your subsequent Turns, Entities in the Radius must Succeed a VIT Save or take Damage.
Entities Damaged by this Ability get the Soaked Effect.

Overflow:

  • As a Main-Action, you can fill Radius with water.
  • Entities inside the water will be effected by the Underwater Rules.
  • This can be done only once on this Ability.
  • Once the Stance Ends, all the weter and Soaked Effect done by this Ability will be gone.

Soaked Effect:

  • On Dealing Damage, the Target gets Soaked with water.
  • This Effect Ends after the Entity uses a Main-Action + Bonus-Action to remove it.
  • It will also End if the Ability Ends.

The Stance Breaks only when entering another Stance.
