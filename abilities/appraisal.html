Description

When cast, Touch a Target and aquire basic information.

When casting this Ability you chose a Cast Time, and it determines the rest of the Ability Stats.

Appraising an Entity:

  • When you Appraise an Entity, you can get basic meta information, such as Health, Total XP, ect...
  • You can also get generally known information, such as Species, Size, Weight, ect...
  • The Ability Fails and returns if the Appraised Entity's Total XP is over Appraisal Power

Appraising an Item:

  • When you Appraise an Item, you can get basic information, such as Material, Size, Weight, General Quality, ect...

Appraising Magical Effects:

  • When you Appraise a Magical Effect, you get to know what it does, and how it works.
  • The Ability Fails if the Magical Effect's Tier or Casted ESS is over Appraisal Power / 100
