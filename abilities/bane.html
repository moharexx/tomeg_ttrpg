Bane

Requirements:

  • Spell: 10 CS, (5 Enchantment | 10 Anima | 20 Abjuration)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Ranged, Soul Save, Debuff
  • Main-Action / Continuous

Description

When used, you are put in Bane Stance.
When Entering Bane Stance, you have a Pool of Bane, and choose One Type of Bane to give to the Targets.
You can Bane Sensed Targets, but Spend no more than 2 × ESS from the Pool on a single Target.
Entities That you Target Must make a Soul Save of 10 × ESS or get the Bane Debuff.

Bane of Incompetence:

  • For each Point of Bane the Target has a Reduction of 2 on Attacks and Mastery-Checks.

Bane of Weakness:

  • For each Point of Bane all Damage Instances from the Target are Reduced by 1, to a Minimum of 0.

The Stance Breaks when: Moving, HD Decrease
