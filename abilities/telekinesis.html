Telekinesis

Requirements:

  • Spell: (10 INT | 10 WILL | 20 DES), (5 Transmutation | 10 Enchantment | 20 Evocation)

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Defense, Utility, Unlocks
  • Physical Damage, Crowd Control

Description

You have Telekinetic Abilities, these Abilities have Action Costs and an Unlock Cost.
You can use the Interact Action with Telekinesis.
When interacting, lifting, and moving inanimate object you take the Force's STR into account, dividing it if there are more Objects.
When you Unlock this Ability, you get 1 of the Unlocks for Free.

Grab (5 XP):

  • As a Free-Action, you can Grab inanimate Objects you can Pinpoint in Range.
  • As a Main-Action, Grab an Entity if they Fail a CS (STR | AGI) Save
  • A Grabbed Entity is Rooted.
  • The Move Unlock and Crush Unlock automatically Succeeds against Grabbed Targets.
  • The Entity can repeat the Save as a Bonus-Action.

Move (5 XP):

  • Moving inanimate Objects is a Free-Action.
  • If you move an Entity, it will costs a Main-Action.
  • Move Distance: 3 × ESS^(1/3)
  • A Target not already Grabbed will throw a CS (STR | AGI) Save against this.

Eject (5 XP):

  • As a Main-Action you can Eject an inanimate Object in Range, this is a Projectile Attack.
  • Eject-Range: 10 / 20 × CS^(1/3)
  • Damage: (2d10 × ESS) Physical

Crush (5 XP):

  • As a Main-Action you can attempt to Crush a Target.
  • Damage: (2d8 × ESS) Physical
  • A Target not already Grabbed will throw a CS (STR | AGI) Save against this.

Projectile Grab (5 XP):

  • As a Main-Action Reaction you attempt to catch a Projectile midair in Range.
  • Grab Strength: 2d10 × ESS
  • If Grab Strength is equal or higher than To-Hit, the Projectile is Grabbed.
  • If not, the Projectile Attack is unchanged.
