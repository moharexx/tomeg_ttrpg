Weapon Throw

Requirements:

  • 5 Melee Weapon | 50 Ballistic | 10 Martial Arts

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Variable Damage, Projectile, AOE
  • Main-Action

Description

When using this Ability, you throw your Melee Weapon.

  • If the Weapon has the Throw Property, the Attack counts as a Line AOE Attack, without the Long Range part.
  • If the Weapon has the Heavy Property, the Attack has a Radius.
  • This Attack Retains Armor-Pen and all Special Properties the Parent Weapon does on a Regular Attack-Action.

Boulder Throw (50 STR | Any Earth Spell):

  • If you are not Dual-Wielding, you can grab a portion of the sorrounding terrain to use as the Projectile.
  • The Object can be: Light | Medium | Heavy

Return (50 WILL):

  • It fucking returns. (Free-Action)
  • This feature can be used in itself as a Bonus-Action to retrieve any Object weighing at Max ESS Kg from 20 × ESS^(1/3) meters
  • The Wielder of the Object is eligable to a STR Save of 4 × ESS
