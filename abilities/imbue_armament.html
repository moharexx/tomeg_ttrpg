Imbue Armament

Requirements:

  • Innate: 50 WILL
  • Spell: (10 INT | 10 WILL | 20 EGO), (5 Enchantment | 10 Transmutation | 20 Anima)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Touch, Buff
  • Bonus-Action

Description

When you use this Ability, you change an Armament's Damage Type.
choose a Type of Imbuement, and if it has a Side Effect, choose one as well.
When Cast on an Armament, change 1 or more of the Armament Damage Types to other ones.
The Weapon's Armor-Pen Stat Will Always match the Armament's new Damage Type.
This Ability must be Cast at least at the Armament's Tier or it will Fail!
Any Side Effect only applies when using the specific Damage Type.

If Cast as Innate, the Ability's Range is Self.
This means only Weapons currently Wielded by You, and Ends if you no longer Wield them.

Type 1 Imbument:

  • List of Damage Types: Physical, Heat, Cold, Bio, Demat, Thunder, Psychic
  • No Side Effects.
  • Electric can be chosen, but it will NOT Maximalize it's Damage against Soaked or Airborne Targets.
  • Radiant or Necrotic can be chosen, but it will deal ONLY either the Physical or the SP part of the Damage.

Type 2 Imbument:

  • List of Damage Types: Electric
  • List of Side Effects:
  • All Abilities with the Weapon Damage Tag have Halved To-Hit, including Attack-Actions.
  • Costs an Extra Bonus-Action to use any Ability with the Weapon Damage Tag, including Attack-Actions.
  • The Weapon Loses 2 × ESS Penetration, the Weapon must have at least 2 × ESS Armor-Pen or this CANNOT be chosen!

Type 3 Imbument:

  • List of Damage Types: Radiant, Necrotic
  • List of Side Effects:
  • Costs an Extra Bonus-Action to use any Ability with the Weapon Damage Tag, including Attack-Actions.
  • The Weapon Losses 4 × ESS Penetration, the Weapon must have at least 4 × ESS Armor-Pen or this CANNOT be chosen!

COMBO:

  • When you use Switch Weapon as a Bonus-Action, you can Cast This Abilty as a Free-Action.
  • Using this Ability you can Target All Weapons currently Wielded by You.
  • When you Cast Enchant Armament Ability, you can Cast This Abilty as a Free-Action.
