Imbue Armament

Requirements:

  • Innate: 20 WILL
  • Spell: 10 CS, (5 Enchantment | 10 Transmutation | 20 Anima)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Touch, Buff
  • Bonus-Action

Description

When you use this Ability, you change an Armament's Damage or Resistance Type.
For each Damage or Resistance Type an Armament has choose an Imbuement.
A Weapon's Armor-Pen Stat will turn into the new Damage Types' Penetration.
Side Effects only apply when the select Damage Type is used.
This Ability must be Cast at least at the Armament's Tier or it will Fail!

If Cast as Innate, the Ability can only Target currently Wielded and Worn Items, and Ends when Un-Wielded or taken off.

Elemental Imbuement (Weapons):

  • List of Damage Types: Physical | Heat | Cold | Bio | Demat | Thunder | Electric | Psychic | Radiant | Necrotic
  • When Electric is chosen, it will NOT Maximize its Damage against Soaked Airborne Targets.
  • When Radiant or Necrotic is chosen, it will deal ONLY either the HP or the SP part of the Damage.

Full Electric Imbuement (Weapons):

  • Change the Damage Type to Electric, which is Maximized against Soaked and Airborne Targets.
  • Choose 1 Side Effect:
  • All Abilities with the Weapon Damage Tag have Halved To-Hit, including Attack-Actions.
  • The Weapon loses 2 × ESS Armor-Pen. (The Weapon must have at least that much Armor-Pen to choose this!)
  • It Costs an Extra Bonus-Action to use any Ability with the Weapon Damage Tag, including Attack-Actions.

Full Hybrid Imbuement (Weapons):

  • List of Damage Types: Radiant, Necrotic
  • Radiant and Necrotic are Hybrid Damage Types, which go both into HP and SP.
  • Choose 1 Side Effect:
  • The Weapon loses 4 × ESS Armor-Pen. (The Weapon must have at least that much Armor-Pen to choose this!)
  • It Costs an Extra Bonus-Action to use any Ability with the Weapon Damage Tag, including Attack-Actions.

Resistance Imbument (Armors):

  • List of Resistance Types: Physical | Heat | Cold | Bio | Demat | Thunder | Electric | Psychic | Radiant | Necrotic

COMBO:

  • This Ability can be used as a Main-Action to Target up to 3 Items instead of 1.
  • When multiple Items are Targeted, the Enchants can be different.
  • When you use Switch_action as a Bonus-Action, you can Cast this Abilty as a Free-Action on one Wielded Item.
  • When you Cast Battle Aura, you can Cast this Abilty as a Free-Action on it.
  • When you Cast Enchant Armament, you can Cast this Abilty as a Free-Action on one of the same Armaments.
