Dexterity Bank

Requirements:

  • Innate: 20 AGI
  • Skill: 10 Acrobatics

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Defense
  • Negation, Self, HD
  • Free-Action, HD

Description

You either take a HD Loan or a HD Interest
HD Gained or Spent does NOT interact with Abilities, like Breaking Stances, ect...

Loan:

  • You gain (20 × ESS) HD but lose (25 × ESS) HD at the End of Your Next Turn.
  • If you cannot pay back the Loan your HD drops to 0, and you gain the Staggered Condition.

Interest:

  • You lose (10 × ESS) HD but gain (20 × ESS) HD at the Start of Your Next Turn.
