Cold Shoulder

Requirements:

  • Skill: 20 Martial Arts
  • Spell: (10 INT | 10 WILL | 10 EGO), (5 Abjuration | 10 Transmutation | 20 Evocation)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Cold Damage, VIT Save, Touch, Crowd Control
  • Main-Action

Description

When used, Touch an Entity while imbued with Cold.
This is an Attack, which deals Cold Damage, and potentionally freezes the Target.

Cold Effect:

  • If the Target is Soaked, the Target must Succeed a VIT Save or get the Stunned Condition.
  • The Condition lasts till the End of the Target's Next Turn.
