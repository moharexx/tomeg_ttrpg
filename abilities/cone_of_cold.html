Cone Of Cold

Requirements:

  • Spell: (20 INT | 20 WILL | 20 EGO), (10 Abjuration | 20 Transmutation | 50 Evocation)

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Ranged, Flow Cone AOE, VIT Save, Cold Damage
  • Main-Action + Bonus-Action

Description

When used, shoot a Cone of cold.
This is a Flow Cone AOE Ranged Save, flowing out from the Caster.
All Targets Inside the Cone must make a VIT Save or get Hit.

Cold Effect:

  • Damage against Soaked Targets is Maximalized.
