Bless

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Buff, Targeting
  • Main-Action / Continuous

Description

When used, you are put in Bless Stance.
When Entering Bless Stance, you have a Pool of Blessing, and choose One Type of Blessing to give to the Targets.
At Cast you Bless Sensed Targets, but Spend no more than 3 × ESS from the Pool on a single Target.

Blessing of Mastery:

  • For each Point of Blessing the Target has a Bonus of 1 for Attacks and Mastery-Checks.

Blessing of Power:

  • For each Point of Blessing all Damage Instances from the Target are Increased by 1.

The Stance Breaks when: Moving | HD Decrease
