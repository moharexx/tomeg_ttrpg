Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Robust Stance.
When Entering Robust Stance, and on subsequent Turns, gain BUFFER equal to a 4 × ESS

Stance Trigger (Main-Action):

  • When this Stance is Triggered, gain 12 × ESS BUFFER.
  • This BUFFER lasts 1 Minute (20 Rounds).

The Stance Breaks only when entering any other Stance.

Note: This Ability CANNOT CRIT!
