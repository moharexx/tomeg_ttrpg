Virus

Requirements:

  • Spell: 5 CS, (5 Anima | 10 Enchantment | 20 Transmutation)

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Unlock
  • Ranged, Bio Damage, VIT Save, Effect
  • Main-Action

Description

When used, you Target a Pinpointed Entity in Range, and attempt to infect them.
If the Target fails a VIT Save of 10 × ESS they gain the Poison Effect.
At Cast you choose the kind of Poison Effect you want to inflict them with from the list below.

When you Unlock this Ability, Choose 1 Unlock that you gain for free.

Deadly (5 XP):

  • Poison Effect:
  • The Ability will do (1d8 × ESS) Bio Executing Damage, at the Start of Caster's Next Turn.
  • At Cast, you can add a Bonus-Action to the Cast Time to make the Damage into 1d12 × ESS Executing.
  • This Effect Ends after Dealing Damage.

Plague (5 XP):

  • Poison Effect:
  • The Ability will do (1d8 × ESS) Bio Damage, at the Start of Caster's Next Turn.
  • At Cast, you can add a Bonus-Action to the Cast Time to Target an additional Target.
  • If the Target gets to 0 or less HP from this Effect, you can Re-Cast this Ability as a Bonus-Action Reaction.
  • Getting Entities to 0 or less HP with less Total XP than 10 × ESS will not Activate the above mentioned benefit.
  • This Effect Ends after Dealing Damage.

Potency Boost:

  • For each 3 × ESS points of Potency you can Target an additional Entity.
