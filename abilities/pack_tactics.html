Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Pack Tactics Stance.
While in Pack Tactics Stance for every other ally Entity in Pack Tactics Stance, you gain Bonus # × 1 × ESS Damage on any initial Damage.

The Stance Breaks only when entering any other Stance.
