Pack Tactics

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Self, Variable Damage
  • Bonus-Action / Continuous

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Pack Tactics Stance.
While in Pack Tactics Stance every other ally in Range who is also in Pack Tactics gets an On-Hit Damage Bonus of ESS
The Damage Bonus you can get is Capped at 4 × ESS

The Stance Breaks only when entering any other Stance.
