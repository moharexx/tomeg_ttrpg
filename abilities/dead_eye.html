Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Dead Eye Stance, and Half your MS.
CRIT Range of the Ability increases by your Stacks.
When the Stance is Triggered, you make Ranged Attack-Actions, against a number of different Targets equal to your Stacks.
Max Stack: 6

  • Ballistic Weapons with Reload or Slow-Reload Property cannot be used.

The Stance Breaks when: Using Abilities, HD Decrease, Taking Damage

"It's high noon."
