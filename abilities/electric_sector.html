Electric Sector

Requirements:

  • Spell: 10 CS, (5 Evocation | 10 Conjuration | 20 Transmutation)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Ranged, Static Sphere AOE, Electric Damage, VIT Save
  • Main-Action / Continuous

Description

Within Range, in a choosen Sphere the electric charge increases, sparkles and bolts of electricity start to appear.
When using this Ability, you are put in Electric Sector Stance.
When Entering this Stance and at the Start of your subsequent Turns, Entities in the Radius must Succeed a VIT Save or take Damage.

On Stance Trigger:

  • As a Main-Action, you can increase it's Damage to 1d12 × ESS
  • This can be done only once on this Ability.

The Stance Breaks only when entering another Stance.
