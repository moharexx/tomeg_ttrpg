Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Titans Slash Stance.
On your Next Turn you can Trigger the Stance.
On Trigger you make an AOE Melee Attack, Hit Entities take Damage.
The AOE Flows from one side edge to the other, like left to right.

The Stance Breaks when: Using Abilities, Moving, HD Decrease, Taking Damage
