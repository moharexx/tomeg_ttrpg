Toxic Wasteland

Requirements:

  • Spell: 10 CS, (5 Anima | 10 Abjuration | 20 Enchantment)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Ranged, Static Sphere AOE, Bio Damage, VIT Save, Executing
  • Main-Action / Continuous

Description

Within Range, in a choosen Sphere noxious gas appear which rots Entities.
When using this Ability, you are put in Toxic Wasteland Stance.
When Entering this Stance and at the Start of your subsequent Turns, Entities in the Radius must Succeed a VIT Save or take Damage.

Overflow:

  • As a Main-Action, you can add Executing to this Ability.
  • This can be done only once on this Ability.

The Stance Breaks only when entering another Stance.
