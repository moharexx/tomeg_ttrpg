Wrathful Smite

Requirements:

  • 20 Anima

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Melee, Necrotic Damage, To-Hit
  • Bonus-Action, SP

Description

You imbue your Melee Weapon with a part of your Soul and wrath for more power.
You add Bonus To-Hit and On-Hit Damage to the Parent Melee Attack by Spending SP.
After using this Ability, the Caster can only use their Actions in the following ways:
Move (Towards) | Attack-Action | Wrathful Smite against/towards the same Entity until the End of the Casters Next Turn.
