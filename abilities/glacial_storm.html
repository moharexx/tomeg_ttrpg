Description

Within Range, in a choosen Sphere the temperature drops, snow and ice starts to appear, in the air and on objects.
When using this Ability, you are put in Glacial Storm Stance.
When Entering this Stance and at the Start of your subsequent Turns, Entities in the Radius must Succeed a VIT Save or take Damage.
Entities Damaged by this Ability get the Cold Effect.

On Stance Trigger:

  • You can Double it's Radius
  • This can be done only once on this Ability.
  • This Stance does NOT Break when you Trigger it.

Cold Effect:

  • The Target's MS is reduced by CS^(1/3)
  • Slow Strength is Doubled against Soaked Targets.
  • Targets reduced to 0 MS count as Rooted.
  • The Cold Effect Ends at the End of Target's Next Turn.

The Stance Breaks only when entering another Stance.
