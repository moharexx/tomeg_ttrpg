Enchant Armament

Requirements:

  • Innate: 20 WILL
  • Spell: 10 CS, (5 Enchantment | 10 Transmutation | 20 Anima)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Utility, Unlock
  • Touch, Buff
  • Bonus-Action

Description

When you use this Ability, you Enchant an Armament.
Spend up to 2 × ESS Enchantment Points (EP for short) on the Targeted Armament(s).
At Cast you allocate the EP to the Armament(s), which lasts for the Duration; 1 Minute (20 Rounds)
Different Enchantments have different Caps on how much EP can be put into them.

There are 3 Slots which can give an active Enchant Buff; The 2 Wielding Slots, and the Armor Slot.
This means at most 3 Active Instances of Enchant Armament on an Entity.
An Item can only have up to 2 Instances of Enchant Armament with the Permanence Meta-Ability.
When using the Switch-Action, you can also change which Enchant Armament Instances you use.
Casting a new Enchant Armament Instance on an Item allows the User of that Item to change to that Instance.
Multiple of the same Enchant Types can NOT be Active at the same time, even if it's different Instances of this Ability!
Note: When using 2 Wielding Slots on a single Item, it can have 2 Active Enchant Armament Instances.

When you Unlock this Ability, Choose 1 Enchant that you own for free, without the XP Cost.
If Cast as Innate, the Ability can only Target currently Wielded and Worn Items, and Ends when Un-Wielded or taken off.

Tier Up (Any Armament) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • The Armament's Tier increases to EP
  • EP Cap: ESS

Damage (Wielding) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • For each Point of EP allocate 1 Damage Bonus.
  • Damage Types: Physical | Heat | Cold | Bio | Demat | Thunder | Electric | Psychic | Radiant | Necrotic
  • This Damage Bonus can apply to Attack-Actions, and Damage Instances which deals the same Type.
  • For Radiant and Necrotic, either Half the Damage Bonus or choose only the HP or SP part of it.
  • EP Cap: 2 × ESS

Support (Wielding) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • For each Point of EP allocate 1 Potency Bonus.
  • Potency Types: Healing | Shielding | Negation
  • Healing Potency can only be Spent on Healing. Duh!
  • Shielding Potency can only be Spent on BUFFERs, or Abilities with the Shielding Tag.
  • Negating Potency can only be Spent on Abilities with the Negation Tag.
  • EP Cap: 2 × ESS

Piercing (Wielding) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • Gain EP extra Penetration on each Damage Instance.
  • EP Cap: 4 × ESS

Accuracy (Wielding) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • Gain a To-Hit Bonus of EP on all Attacks.
  • EP Cap: 4 × ESS

Reach | Range (Melee Weapon | Ranged Weapon) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • This Enchantment, unlike the others, Costs a set amount per Level, and can be Upgraded at Max 3 Times.
  • A Melee Weapon's Reach Increases by ESS^(1/3) per Level.
  • A Ranged Weapon's Range Increases by (20 / 40) × ESS^(1/3) per Level.
  • EP Cost Per Level: ESS
  • Level Cap: 3

Agile (Wielding | Armor) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • Increase Max HD by: 5 × EP
  • EP Cap: 4 × ESS

Dodging (Wielding | Armor) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • Incoming Attacks' Finalized To-Hit is reduced by EP
  • EP Cap: 4 × ESS

Armor (Armor) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • For each Point of EP put into this, allocate 9 Points of Resistance to the Armor.
  • Resistance Types: Physical | Heat | Cold | Bio | Demat | Thunder | Electric | Psychic | Radiant | Necrotic
  • All Resistance Types are Capped at Armor's Property Points × Tier, this Cap includes the Armor's Base Resistances.
  • EP Cap: Unlimited

Resiliance (Wielding | Armor) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • Gain EP Armor VS All.
  • EP Cap: 2 × ESS

COMBO:

  • This Ability can be used as a Main-Action to Target up to 3 Items instead of 1.
  • When multiple Items are Targeted, the Enchants can be different.
  • When you use Switch_action as a Bonus-Action, you can Cast this Abilty as a Free-Action on one Wielded Item.
  • When you Cast Battle Aura, you can Cast this Abilty as a Free-Action on it.
  • When you Cast Imbue Armament, you can Cast this Abilty as a Free-Action on one of the same Armaments.

Potency Boost:

  • For each point of Potency you can have +1 EP.
  • When multiple Items are Targeted, rather than all receiving +EP, you have to spread out the +EP between Items.
  • Note: Potency Effects fade after 1 Minute (20 Rounds), even if the Ability would last longer!
