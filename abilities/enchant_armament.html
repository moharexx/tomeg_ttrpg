Enchant Armament

Requirements:

  • Innate: 50 WILL
  • Spell: 10 CS, (5 Enchantment | 10 Transmutation | 20 Anima)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Utility, Unlock
  • Touch, Buff
  • Bonus-Action

Description

When you use this Ability, you Enchant an Armament.
You Gain 3 × ESS Enchantment Points, EP for short.
At Cast you allocate the EP on Buffs to the Targeted Armament, which lasts for the Duration.
Different Enchantments have different Caps on how much EP can be put into them.

When you Unlock this Ability, Choose 1 Unlock that you gain for free.
If Cast as Innate, the Ability can only Target currently Wielded and Worn Armaments, and Ends when Unequipped.

Tier Up (Any Armament) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • The Armament's Tier increases to EP
  • EP Cap: EP

Damage (Anything Wieldable) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • Choose 1 Damage Type; Physical | Heat | Cold | Bio | Demat | Thunder | Electric | Psychic | Radiant | Necrotic
  • While Wielding, you can choose to gain a EP Damage Bonus.
  • This Damage Bonus can apply to any Attack-Action, or any Damage Instance which deals the same Type.
  • When Radiant or Necrotic is chosen, either Half the Damage Bonus or only choose the HP or SP part of it.
  • If the User could benefit from multiple sources of this, choose only one!
  • EP Cap: 2 × ESS

Support (Anything Wieldable) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • Choose 1 Type; Healing | Shielding
  • While Wielding, you gain EP extra Potency.
  • When choosing Healing, this Potency can only be Spent on Healing. Duh!
  • When choosing Shielding, this Potency can only be Spent on BUFFERs, or Abilities with the Shielding Tag.
  • If the User could benefit from multiple sources of this, choose only one!
  • EP Cap: 2 × ESS

Piercing (Any Weapon) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • The Weapon gains EP extra Armor-Pen.
  • EP Cap: 3 × ESS

Accuracy (Anything Wieldable) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • While Wielding gain a Bonus To-Hit of EP on all Attacks.
  • EP Cap: 3 × ESS

Reach | Range (Melee Weapon | Ranged Weapon) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • This Enchantment, unlike the others, Costs a set amount per Level, and can be Upgraded at Max 3 Times.
  • A Melee Weapon's Reach Increases by ESS^(1/3) per Level.
  • A Ranged Weapon's Range Increases by (20 / 40 × ESS^(1/3)) per Level.
  • EP Cost Per Level: ESS
  • Level Cap: 3

Agile (Anything Wieldable | Armor) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • While Wielding or Wearing the Item, the User's Max HD increases by: 5 × EP
  • If the User could benefit from multiple sources of this, choose only one!
  • EP Cap: 3 × ESS

Dodging (Shield | Armor) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • While Wielding or Wearing the Item, any incoming Attack with To-Hit under 2 × EP is treated as 0.
  • Note: This looks at Finalized To-Hit, so after all modifiers, like Disadvantage!
  • If the User could benefit from multiple sources of this, choose only one!
  • EP Cap: 3 × ESS

Armor (Armor) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • For each Point of EP put into this, allocate 6 Points or Resistance to the Armor.
  • Resistance Types; Physical | Heat | Cold | Bio | Demat | Thunder | Electric | Psychic | Radiant | Necrotic
  • All Resistance Types are Capped at Armor's Property Points × Tier, this includes the Armor's Base Resistance.
  • If the User could benefit from multiple sources of this, choose only one!
  • EP Cap: Unlimited

Resiliance (Anything Wieldable | Armor) Unlock: 5 XP:

  • For each 3 Point of EP put into this, gain 2 Armor VS All.
  • You gain this Bonus only while Wielding the Item or Wearing the Armor.
  • If the User could benefit from multiple sources of this, choose only one!
  • EP Cap: 6 × ESS

COMBO:

  • When multiple Armaments are Targeted, the Buffs can be different.
  • This Ability can be used as a Main-Action to Target up to 3 Items instead of 1.
  • When you use Switch Weapon as a Bonus-Action, you can Cast this Abilty as a Free-Action on one Wielded Weapon.
  • When you Cast the Battle Aura Ability, you can Cast this Abilty as a Free-Action on it.
  • When you Cast the Imbue Armament Ability, you can Cast this Abilty as a Free-Action on one of the same Armaments.

Potency Boost:

  • For each point of Potency you can have +1 EP.
  • Note: Potency Effects fade after 1 Minute (20 Rounds), even if the Ability would last longer!
