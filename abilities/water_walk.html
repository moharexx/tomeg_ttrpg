Water Walk

Requirements:

  • Spell: (5 INT | 5 WILL | 5 EGO), (5 Enchantment | 10 Transmutation)

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Touch, Mobility, Buff
  • Main-Action

Description

When used, the Target Object or Entity will stay on top of any liquid, like if it was a solid surface.
Unwilling Targets can make a Soul Save (12 × ESS) to not be Affected.
When falling onto a liquid surface, fall damage is same as falling onto a solid.
If the surface of the liquid has larger waves, at the Start of the Target's Turn it must succeed on a STR Save 10 or get the Prone Condition.
If the Target is already submerged it will rush to the surface at 10 × ESS^(1/3) meters, at each End of their Turns.
Everytime your Target tries to swim deeper by any means it must make an Athletics Check of (20 × ESS), or Fail.
Note that the Liquid is still in contact with the Target, and can still hurt the Target.

Jesus (5 XP):

  • If cast on Self, Duration is Permanent, and Toggleable as a Free-Action Reaction.
