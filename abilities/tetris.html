Tetris

Requirements:

  • Skill: 200 Melee Weapon | 200 Martial Arts

Unlock Cost:

  • 200 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Defense
  • Weapon Damage, Flow Line AOE, Mobility, HD
  • Bonus-Action / Continuous

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Tetris Stance.
While in Tetris Stance you can perform different Tricks.
If you don't have the Stacks to Spend you cannot do the Trick, duh!
These Tricks count as Abilities, unless said otherwise.
Max Stack: 4

Upstack (Bonus-Action):

  • Gain 1 Stack, and remove the Prone Condition from Yourself.
  • Using the Upstack Trick does NOT count as an Ability.

Downstack (Bonus-Action):

  • Spend 1 Stack, gain (15 × ESS) HD

Tetris (Bonus-Action):

  • Spend 1 Stack, do a Flow Line AOE Melee Attack.
  • Line Length: 4 × ESS^(1/3)
  • Line width: ESS^(1/3)
  • Damage: (2d8 × ESS) Weapon Damage Type

T-Spin (Bonus-Action):

  • Spend 1 Stack, perform a Move-Action then a Melee or Ranged Attack-Action.
  • This can only be used once per Round.

All-Spin (Bonus-Action):

  • Spend 2 Stacks, gain (15 × ESS) HD and perform a Move-Action.
  • This Move-Action does NOT Activate Reactions and Triggers.

Perfect Clear (Bonus-Action):

  • Can only be used at exactly 3 Stacks, and Ends the Stance!
  • Spend 3 Stacks, do an Executing Melee Attack, with Melee Reach.
  • Damage: (2d6 × ESS) Weapon Damage Type
  • Penetration: 10 × ESS

You can drop to 0 Stacks with this Stance, and that won't Break it.
The Stance Breaks when Taking Damage while at 3 or more Stacks.
This Stance Breaking gives you the Staggered Condition.

"Have you ever played Tetris with your life on the line?"
