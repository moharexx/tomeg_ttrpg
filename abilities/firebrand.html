Tags:

Description

You imbue your Melee Attack with Fire.
On a Hit you deal additional (1d12 × ESS) Heat Damage and inflict the Heat Effect.

Heat Effect:

  • At the Start of the Caster's Next Turn the Target takes (1d12 × ESS) Heat Damage.
  • This Effect Ends after Dealing Damage.
