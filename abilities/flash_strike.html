Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Flash Strike Stance.
The Next Turn you can Trigger the Flash Strike.
On Trigger you flash in a Flow Line AOE Melee Attack, you are the Flow of the AOE.
This means when your Hit is Fully Negated you stop midway.

The Stance Breaks when: Using Abilities, Moving, HD Decrease, Taking Damage
