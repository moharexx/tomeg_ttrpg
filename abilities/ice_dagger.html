Ice Dagger

Requirements:

  • Skill: 10 Melee Weapon

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Cold Damage, Executing
  • Main-Action

Description

Add On-Hit inflict a Cold Effect onto the Target.

Cold Effect:

  • At the Start of Caster's Next Turn the Target recieves 2d6 × ESS Executing Cold Damage.
  • After the Damage the Cold Effect Ends.

Note: A Cold Effect can be removed by a Heat Effect.
