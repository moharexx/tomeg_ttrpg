Supernatural Flight

Requirements:

  • Innate: 2000 Total XP

Unlock Cost:

  • 200 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Mobility

Description

You can Fly, why? Because this Ability says so.
You can do all movement things while not on the ground, and do the Move-Action and every other Movement in any direction, ignoring gravity.
If you wish you don't Fall, simply staying afloat, standing in the air or whatever, go out and aurafarm.

You are NOT considered as Airborne for any Condition interaction.
You can still get the Prone Condition, imagined as a loss of balance.
While you have the Prone or Staggered Condition, or are Sprinting, you CAN have the Airborne Condition!
