Gas Screen

Requirements:

  • Spell: (10 INT | 10 WILL | 10 EGO), (5 Conjuration | 10 Transmutation | 20 Enchantment)

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Utility, Unlock
  • Bio Damage, Crawl Cone AOE, Obscurance
  • Main-Action

Description

When used, you create a Poisonous Gas in a Cone, that Sight based Perception cannot see through.
Entities in the Gas at the Start of Their Turns must make a VIT Save or take Damage.
When a Wind based Attack is used, if it Deals more than 20 × ESS Damage it disperses the Gas Screen.
If the Wind is an AOE then 5 × ESS Damage is Enough to disperse the Gas Screen.

Shaped Cloud (50 Transmutation)

  • This Ability can be Shaped in whatever way the Caster desires.
