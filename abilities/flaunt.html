Flaunt

Requirements:

  • Skill: 10 Performance

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Self, Buff
  • 1 Minute / Continuous

Description

When using this Ability, you are put in Flaunt Stance.
While in Flaunt Stance Performance Checks gain a Bonus of 10 × ESS
While in Flaunt Stance you can do 1 Bonus-Action Feint as a Free-Action.

  • This Ability Stays on even when Unconscious.

The Stance Breaks only when entering any other Stance.
