Shield Bash

Requirements:

  • 10 Melee Weapon

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Debuff, Mobility
  • Main-Action

Description

When you use this Ability, you Dash and Bash your Shield into an Entity.
The Entity that you Bash gets Dazed Condition on a Failed STR Save.
The Dazed Condition lasts till End of Target's Next Turn.

Using this Ability with a Heavy Shield:

  • The Ability STR Save is 12 × ESS But The Dash Distance is MS / 2
