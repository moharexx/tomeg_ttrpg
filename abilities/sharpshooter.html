Sharpshooter

Requirements:

  • Innate: 5 AGI, 5 CS

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Buff, Mastery

Description

You can ignore Ballistic Mastery's AGI requirement, and can put it up to Total XP / 10
Ballistic Mastery's Modifier can be Ballistic Mastery, Capped at Total XP / 20
For Ranged Abilities (Including Attack-Actions), you can replace AGI Scalings with AGI + CS, Capped at Total XP / 10
