Sharpshooter

Requirements:

  • 5 AGI, (5 INT | 5 WILL | 5 EGO)

Unlock Cost:

  • 5 XP

Tags:

  • Offense
  • Mastery

Description

The Maximum Mastery you can put into Ballistics can be equal to your AGI + CS, to a Maximum of Total XP / 10
Ballistic Mastery's Modifier (and To-Hit) can be (AGI | CS) / 4 + Ballistic / 2, to a Maximum of Total XP / 20
For Ranged Abilities (Including Attack-Actions) you can replace AGI Scalings with AGI + CS + Ballistic Mastery, to a Maximum of Total XP / 10

Note: This Ability cannot result in higher stats than a maxxed character without it, hence every formula is limited by Total XP.
So like if someone maxxes AGI and Ballistic, you cannot have higher To-Hit at the same Total XP with this Ability.
