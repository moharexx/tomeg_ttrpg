Shield Roar

Requirements:

  • 10 Melee Weapon

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Offense, Defense
  • Flow Sphere AOE, Crowd Control
  • Bonus-Action

Description

When you Fully Negate an Incoming Hit, you can use this Ability to frighten your enemies around you.
Targets, Excluding your Allies, within the Radius make a Soul Save or get the Terror Effect.

  • When this Ability is Used with a Heavy Shield, it's Soul Save is 6 × ESS, but it's Radius is Doubled.

Terror Effect:

  • Entities have Disadvantage on Attacks against the Caster and cannot willfully move closer to the Caster.
  • The Target can Repeat the Save for this Effect by using a Main-Action.
  • This Effect Ends if the Caster gets the Dying Condition or Dies.
  • Effect Duration: 1 Minute (20 Rounds)

This Ability cannot be used without a Shield Equipped.
