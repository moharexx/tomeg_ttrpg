Essence Sustenance

Requirements:

  • Innate: 20 VIT | 20 Anima

Unlock Cost:

  • 20 XP

Tags:

  • Utility, Unlock
  • Survival

Description

You can live without food, water, air and sleep.

Divine Body (50 VIT | 50 Anima):

  • You don't need to release any material from your body. (waste, sweat, skin oil, ect...)
