Universal Compass

Requirements:

  • Innate: 200 Intuition | 20 Divination

Unlock Cost:

  • 10 XP

Tags:

  • Utility
  • Mastery, Information

Description

You have a Universal Compass built into your mind.
You can do Sense Nature using Divination.
You have an inbuilt clock, tracking time precisely, you need not Roll for determining Time.
You know the exact time passed since anything you can remember.
You have an inbuilt gyroscope, tracking orientation precisely, you need not Roll for determining Orientation.
You know the exact path you took since anything you can remember.
You can use Sense Nature on settlements and buildings, like finding a tavern, a nearby village, ect...
